  UCSC in the News

<p>
  Psychology's <b>Craig Haney</b> was featured in an article in <i>The Nation</i> about supermax prisons.
</p>
<p>
  <b>Francisco Hernandez,</b> vice chancellor for Student Affairs, was quoted in an article in <i>BusinessWire.com</i> about the AP test preparation service now being offered on the web free to California high school students by the University of California. The service is an extension of the <a href="http://www.uccp.org">UC College Prep Initiative,</a> which Hernandez developed in collaboration with other UC campuses.
</p>

</p>
