<p>
  For the month of December, the Bay Tree Bookstore will offer a 20 percent discount on all general books, including best sellers and new releases. Sale items and special orders are excluded.
</p>
<p>
  The bookstore is also having a special one-day holiday sale on Wednesday, December 15, from 8:30 a.m. to 5:30 p.m. There will be a 20 percent discount on all UCSC apparel, insignia, ornaments, and plush gift items. Light refreshments will be offered throughout the day. The sale excludes items previously marked down or on sale.<br>
</p><br>
