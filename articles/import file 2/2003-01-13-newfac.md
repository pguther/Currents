  New Faculty
</p>
<p>
  <span class="sectionhead">Athanasios Kottas</span><br>
  Assistant Professor of Applied Math and Statistics
</p>
<p>
  Athanasios Kottas's research interests include Bayesian modeling and inference, Bayesian nonparametrics, survival analysis, semiparametric regression modeling, spatial statistics, and categorical data analysis. Kottas earned B.S. and M.S. degrees in mathematics from the University of Ioannina, Greece, and a Ph.D. in statistics from the University of Connecticut. Before joining the UCSC faculty, he was a visiting assistant professor at Duke University.
</p>
<p>
  <span class="sectionhead">Herbert Lee</span><br>
  Assistant Professor of Applied Math and Statistics<br>
  <br>
  Herbert Lee's research interests include Bayesian statistics, computational statistics, inverse problems, nonparametric regression, and neural networks. Lee earned a B.S. in mathematics from Yale University and M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in statistics from Carnegie Mellon University. Before coming to UCSC he was a visiting assistant professor at Duke University.<br>
</p>
