<p class="pagehead">
  UCSC in the News
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Chicago Tribune</i> ran a story about the return of three sea lions to Brookfield Zoo after more than three years at Long Marine Lab, where they took part in research by <b>Terrie Williams,</b> professor of ecology and evolutionary biology. Williams and head trainer <b>Brett Long</b> were both quoted in the article.<br>
</p>
<p>
  Literature professor <b>Paul Skenazy</b> contributed several book reviews to the <i>Washington Post.</i><br>
</p>
<p>
  A story in <i>Good Times</i> about genomic research at UCSC featured computer science professor <b>David Haussler,</b> research scientist <b>Jim Kent,</b> and graduate student <b>Robert Baertsch.</b> The researchers are studying the genetic similarities and differences between humans and other animals.
</p>
<p>
  Professor of history <b>Dana Frank</b> was quoted in an <i>Orange County Register</i> article about the first-ever academic conference devoted entirely to discussing and analyzing Wal-Mart and its effect on society.
</p>
<p>
  Film and digital media professor and department chair <b>Chip Lord</b> was quoted in a <i>Los Angeles Times</i> article about the Ant Farm, the innovative architecture collective he cofounded in 1968.<br>
</p>
