<a name="content" id="content"></a>
<p>
  Associate professor of history <strong>David Anthony</strong> was the featured guest in a segment of NPR's <i>To The Point</i>, where he discussed his book <i>Max Yergan: Race Man, Internationalist, Cold Warrior</i>. A review of Anthony's book also appeared in <i>Against the Current</i> magazine.
</p>
<p>
  Astronomers <b>Raja Guhathakurta</b> and <b>Jason Kalirai</b> were quoted in stories about their discovery of a large stellar halo around the galaxy Andromeda that ran on many news sites, including MSNBC, CNN, UPI, BBC News, CBC News (Canada), Discovery Channel News, and Space.com.
</p>
<p>
  Film scholar <strong>B. Ruby Rich</strong> of community studies was quoted in an article about Cybill Shepherd's decision to play a lesbian on Showtime's show <i>L Word.</i> The article appeared in the <i>Philadelphia Inquirer</i>.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Mid-County Post</i> ran an article about research by astronomers <b>Piero Madau</b> and <b>Jurg Diemand</b> on the dark matter halo of the Milky Way.<br>
</p>
<p>
  <strong>Daniel Wirls</strong> of politics penned an opinion piece for the <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> that detailed the enormous military buildup that has taken place during President Bush's tenure in the White House.
</p>
<p>
  Economist <strong>Nirvikar Singh</strong> penned an opinion piece about trends in management education in the United States and elsewhere that appeared in <i>Indian Express</i>.
</p>
<p>
  Ethical financial advisers use some of the same manipulative tactics as unscrupulous salespeople and con artists, according to social psychologist <strong>Anthony Pratkanis</strong>, who was quoted in an Associated Press article on the subject.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Monterey County Herald</i> ran a long article about the gardens and programs of the <b>UCSC Arboretum</b>.
</p>
<p>
  Salt Lake City's <i>Deseret Morning News</i> noted that history of consciousness professor <strong>Angela Davis</strong> would be the keynote speaker for the 2007 Martin Luther King Jr. celebration at the University of Utah.
</p>
