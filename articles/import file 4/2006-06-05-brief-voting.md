<p>
  Polls will be open from 7 a.m. to 8 p.m. Tuesday for the gubernatorial primary election.
</p>
<p>
  Polling places on campus are:
</p>
<p>
  * Oakes College: Learning Center<br>
  * Cowell College Apartments: Community Room<br>
  * Crown/Merrill Apartments: Community Room<br>
  * Porter College Apartments: I Lounge<br>
  * College Nine: Red Room, Rm 201<br>
  * College Ten: Namaste Lounge<br>
  * Stevenson College: Silverman Conference Room
</p>
<p>
  Additional information is available at the Santa Cruz County <a href="http://www.votescount.com/">Elections Department web site.</a>
</p>
