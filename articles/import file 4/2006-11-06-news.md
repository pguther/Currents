<a name="content" id="content"></a>
<p>
  Acting Chancellor <b>George Blumenthal</b> was interviewed on the KUSP program <i>Talk of the Bay</i> about town-gown relations, student protests, campus growth, and other challenges facing the university (<a href="http://podcasts.ucsc.edu/blumenthal-kusp-interview_10-23-06.mp3">listen</a>).
</p>
<p>
  Astronomer <b>Steven Vogt</b> was featured in an article in <i>Discover</i> magazine about the search for Earthlike planets, and a cover story on planet hunters in <i>Smithsonian</i> magazine includes quotes from astronomer <b>Gregory Laughlin</b>. Both stories were written by <b>Robert Irion</b>, director of UCSC's Science Communication Program.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> ran a full-page feature story on <i>Intimate Politics</i>, the new memoir by nationally renowned feminist studies professor <b>Bettina Aptheker</b>.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> ran a story about astronomer <b>Michael Bolte</b>, who was recently appointed director of UC Observatories/Lick Observatory.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Buffalo News</i> described <i><a href="http://itre.cis.upenn.edu/%7Emyl/languagelog/">Language Log,</a></i> a blog created by linguistics professor <b>Geoffrey Pullum</b> and University of Pennsylvania phonetics professor Mark Liberman, in a story about the linguistic crimes and misdemeanors committed by politicians.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Mid-County Post</i> ran a story about research by environmental toxicologist <b>Donald Smith</b> on lead poisoning in condors.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> quoted Arts &amp; Lectures director <b>Michelle Witt</b> in a story about the new 2006-07 season.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> noted the publication of <i>Bodies in the Making</i>, a new book of essays coedited by UC professors <b>Helene Moglen</b> and <b>Nancy Chen</b>, based on a conference presented by UCSC's Institute for Advanced Feminist Research.
</p>
<p>
  <a href="http://www.ucsc.edu/news_events/media_highlights"><b>Media Highlights</b></a> provides monthly summaries of "UCSC in the News" columns.
</p>
