<a name="content" id="content"></a>
<p>
  Anthropologist <strong>Nate Dominy's</strong> research on the diet of early human ancestors was covered by the <i>Christian Science Monitor</i>.
</p>
<p>
  Research led by planetary scientist <strong>Francis Nimmo</strong> on Saturn's moon Enceladus was featured in online news stories from MSNBC, Fox News, Discovery Channel, <i>New Scientist, National Geographic, Cosmos, Seed</i> magazine, <i>Earthtimes, Science Daily, Technology News Daily, South African Star, Spaceflight Now, Astrobiology News,</i> and <i>Space.com.</i>
</p>
<p>
  Economist <strong>Lori Kletzer</strong> was interviewed by columnist Rick Wartzman of the <i>Los Angeles Times</i> for a column on globalization and its impact on California.
</p>
<p>
  <i>Education Week</i> profiled the <strong>New Teacher Center</strong>, showcasing the mentor-based program that supports new teachers during their first two years in the classroom.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> ran a feature about <strong>Shakespeare Santa Cruz</strong>'s "25-year run of theatrical excellence," quoting theater arts professor <strong>Paul Whitworth</strong>--now in his final season as artistic director--as well as professor emerita of theater arts and founding artistic director <strong>Audrey Stanley</strong>, and adjunct theater arts professor and former artistic director <strong>Michael Edwards</strong>.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Silicon Valley/San Jose Business Journal</i> ran a story about the appointment of <strong>Phillip Berman</strong> to the Baskin Chair in Biomolecular Engineering.
</p>
<p>
  <strong>Susan Harding</strong> of anthropology fielded a call from the <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> following the death of Jerry Falwell.
</p>
<p>
  Ocean scientists <strong>Raphael Kudela</strong> and <strong>Mary Silver</strong> were featured in coverage of the harmful algal bloom in Monterey Bay by the <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel, Monterey County Herald,</i> and local newscasts on KSBW and KPIX-TV.
</p>
<p>
  Literature professor <strong>Chris Connery</strong> was quoted in a <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> article about a visit by Venezuela's ambassador to UCSC's Chicano Latino Resource Center.
</p>
<p>
  In a feature about popular English scholar Philip Collins, Britain's <i>The Independent</i> noted that the internationally known professor taught at the annual <strong>Dickens Universe</strong> conference held each summer at UCSC.
</p>

</p>
