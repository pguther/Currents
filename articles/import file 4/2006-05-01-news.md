<a name="content" id="content"></a>
<p>
  A front-page story in the <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> on stem cell research featured biologists <b>David Feldheim</b> and <b>Manny Ares</b>, as well as quotes from Chancellor <b>Denice Denton</b> and <b>Ann Pace</b>, assistant director of the Center for Biomolecular Science and Engineering.
</p>
<p>
  Economist <b>Yin-Wong Cheung</b> got the last word--literally--in a <i>U.S. News &amp; World Report</i> article about currency trading, likening individual traders to boxers with "one hand tied behind their back." . . . Also in economics, <b>Michael Dooley</b> was featured in an article in <i>The Economist</i> magazine about the International Monetary Fund.
</p>
<p>
  <i>ABC News</i> featured photographs from the Philip Hyde archive at McHenry Library's <b>Special Collections</b> for a segment of the program <i>This Week</i> hosted by George Stephanopoulos, in honor of the late photographer's impact on the environmental movement. Special Collections also provided images from the Hyde archive for obituaries in the <i>New York Times</i> and the <i>Los Angeles Times</i>, and retired University Librarian <b>Allan Dyson</b> was additionally quoted in the <i>L.A. Times</i> memoriam.
</p>
<p>
  Campus veterinarian <b>David Caspe</b>r was interviewed by a camera crew working on a documentary about gray whales for the National Geographic Channel.
</p>
<p>
  Astrophysicist <b>Stan Woosley</b> was quoted in an article in <i>New Scientist</i> magazine about gamma-ray bursts.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Chronicle of Higher Education</i> ran an interview with history professor <b>Dana Frank</b> about her new book <i>Bananeras: Women Transforming the Banana Unions of Latin America</i>.
</p>
<p>
  The Associated Press picked up an article by the <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> about the organic food program at UCSC, quoting recent graduate and part-time staffer <b>Tim Galarneau</b>.
</p>
<p>
  <i>Metro Santa Cruz</i> published an extensive cover story about UCSCs new Digital Arts and New Media graduate program, quoting assistant theater arts professor <b>Ted Warburton</b>, assistant computer science professor <b>James Davis</b>, assistant professor of art <b>Ed Osborn</b>, and assistant film and digital media professor <b>Warren Sack</b>
</p>

  <a href="http://www.ucsc.edu/news_events/media_highlights"><b>Media Highlights</b></a> provides monthly summaries of "UCSC in the News" columns.
</p>
