<p>
  The <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> ran a front-page story about <i>Then &amp; Now: Santa Cruz Coast</i>, a new book by geologist <b>Gary Griggs</b> and Deepika Shrestha Ross.
</p>
<p>
  Biology graduate student <b>Shaye Wolf</b> is featured in an article in <i>Audubon</i> magazine, along with biologists <b>Donald Croll</b> and <b>Bernie Tershy</b>. The story focuses on their efforts to protect island-nesting seabirds from rats and other nonnative predators.
</p>
