<a name="content" id="content"></a>
<p>
  Biologist <b>Barry Sinervo</b> was featured in an article in the <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> about his research on a salamander--possibly a new species--that lives in the caves on campus.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Mid-County Post</i> and <i>Sea Technology</i> ran stories about research on marine mammals by <b>Daniel Costa</b>, professor of ecology and evolutionary biology, and graduate student <b>Michael Weise</b>.
</p>
<p>
  Research to develop new biosensor technology led by electrical engineer <b>Holger Schmidt</b> was covered in the <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel, Nanotechnology Now</i>, and <i>Laser Focus World</i>.
</p>
<p>
  An article in the <i>International Herald Tribune</i> about the official time standard included quotes from <b>Steven Allen</b>, a programmer/analyst for UCO/Lick Observatory.
</p>
