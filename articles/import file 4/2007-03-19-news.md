<a name="content" id="content"></a>
<p>
  Astronomer <strong>Sandra Faber</strong> was quoted in a <i>Christian Science Monitor</i> story about the AEGIS galaxy survey.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>New York Times</i> quoted assistant professor of film and digital media <strong>L. S. Kim</strong> in an article about the lack of Asian American pop stars.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Chronicle of Higher Education</i> picked up on a paper by psychology's <strong>Jean Fox Tree</strong> about the use of the word "like" in conversation. Rather than a bad habit, the word "like" is used "at particular times and for particular reasons" and can function as an integral part of conversation, according to Fox Tree, whose findings appeared in the journal <i>Discourse Studies</i>.
</p>
<p>
  Computer scientist <strong>Arthur Keller</strong> and graduate student <strong>John Connors</strong> were featured in a <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> story about a campus symposium on electric vehicles.
</p>
<p>
  Biologist <strong>Tim Tinker</strong> was quoted in a <i>Monterey County Weekly</i> story about California sea otters.
</p>
<p>
  Astronomer <strong>Greg Laughlin</strong> was interviewed on KUSP Radio's <i>Talk of the Bay</i> program about his research on extrasolar planets, and was also interviewed by a TV crew from the syndicated program <i>Discoveries &amp; Breakthroughs Inside Science.</i>
</p>
<p>
  Biologist <strong>David Feldheim</strong> was quoted in an article in the journal <i>Nature</i> about stem cell research grants.
</p>
<p>
  Research by biochemists <strong>William Scott</strong> and <strong>Michael Robertson</strong> on the structure of an RNA enzyme was covered by <i>Science Daily, Biology News Net, PhysOrg.com, Chemistry World, Astrobiology News,</i> and <i>Space Ref.</i>
</p>
<p>
  The <i>San Francisco Chronicle</i> announced the new Shakespeare Santa Cruz summer season, quoting artistic director and professor of theater arts <strong>Paul Whitworth</strong>.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Ventura County Star</i> and <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> ran articles about alumnus <strong>Wayne Horvitz</strong>, noting that the individual major he created at UCSC, "Composition for New Music Improvisation," is an apt description of his professional career.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>San Luis Obispo Tribune</i> covered a Women's History Month celebration keynote address by history of consciousness professor <strong>Angela Davis</strong> at Cal Poly, where she spoke of the need for collaborative community approaches to solving issues such as violence against women.
</p><strong>Margaret Gordon</strong><i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i>
