<a name="content" id="content"></a>
<p>
  Cindy Delgado has been appointed to the position of campus workers' compensation claims coordinator.
</p>
<p>
  Delgado has a strong background in workers' compensation subject areas. In addition, she has taken workers' compensation specialty/professional education courses, and she has operated her own vocational rehabilitation consulting business. She is a graduate of San Francisco State University and is fluent in Spanish.
</p>
<p>
  Contact Delgado with questions about workers' comp claim reporting, medical care, and benefits. Kathy McCabe, the transitional return-to-work coordinator, will continue to manage transitional/modified work assignments for employees with occupational injuries. Kelly Roberts, the disability management coordinator, continues to manage cases involving accommodation of permanent disabilities as well as administering non-workers' comp short and long-term disability benefits.
</p>
<p>
  Delgado can be reached at (831) 459-2850 or <a href="mailto:cadelgad@ucsc.edu">cadelgad@ucsc.edu</a>.
</p>
