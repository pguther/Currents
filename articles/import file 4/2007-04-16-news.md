<a name="content" id="content"></a>
<p>
  Linguistics professor <strong>Geoffrey Pullum</strong> appeared on National Public Radio's <i>Weekend Edition</i> in a discussion of the unique language of the Piraha Indians of northwest Brazil.
</p>
<p>
  An article in <i>New Scientist</i> about star clusters near the center of our galaxy included quotes from astronomer <strong>Douglas Lin.</strong>
</p>
<p>
  The <i>San Jose Mercury News</i> ran a story about the UC Santa Cruz Predatory Bird Research Group's web cam on a pair of peregrine falcons nesting at San Jose City Hall, with quotes from biologist <strong>Glenn Stewart,</strong> who was also featured along with Seymour Center director <strong>Julie Heffington</strong> in coverage by KGO TV of the peregrines in San Jose, San Francisco, and at Long Marine Lab.
</p>
<p>
  Astronomer <strong>Stan Woosley</strong> was quoted in a story in <i>Asian News International</i> and <i>Hindustan Times</i> about a rare double explosion of a massive star.
</p>
<p>
  <i>Space Daily</i> ran a story about research on RNA enzymes and the origins of life by biochemist <strong>William Scott.</strong>
</p>
<p>
  <i>Esquire</i> magazine ran an article about 26-year-old art alumnus <strong>Brandon Bird</strong>, who has developed a following for his paintings which depict celebrities in unusual situations.
</p>
