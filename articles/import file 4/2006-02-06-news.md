<a name="content" id="content"></a>
<p>
  An article in the <i>San Jose Mercury News</i> about the effects of global warming in California included quotes from <strong>Lisa Sloan,</strong> professor of Earth sciences, and postdoctoral researcher <strong>Lara Kueppers</strong>, as well as references to several studies conducted by other researchers in Sloan's lab.
</p>
<p>
  <b>Michael Hutchison</b>, economics professor and interim dean of social sciences, was a guest on <i>Forum</i>, KQED Radio's public affairs program, discussing the Japanese economy and the recent stock market fiasco there.
</p>
<p>
  Britain's <i>Guardian Weekly</i> and <i>The Observer</i> quoted film and digital media professor <strong>Chip Lord</strong> for an article about the decline of U.S. automakers. (Lord was a founder of the architectural collective that created <a href="http://currents.ucsc.edu/03-04/01-19/ant_farm.html"><i>Cadillac Ranch</i></a>.)
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Christian Science Monitor</i> relied heavily on comments by economist <strong>Carl Walsh</strong> in its coverage of Alan Greenspan's retirement.
</p>
<p>
  <strong>Craig Haney</strong> of psychology was quoted in a <i>Philadelphia Inquirer</i> article about the mental state of a man on death row, noting the mentally debilitating effects of isolation.
</p>
<p>
  The Turkish publication <i>HurriyetUSA</i> featured an article on music lecturer <strong>Mesut Ozgen</strong> and his "New Dimensions in Classical Guitar" project.
</p>
<p>
  <strong>Jonathan Fox</strong> of Latin American and Latino studies had a letter published in the <i>Wall Street Journal</i> that clarified the sweep of an 1882 law to filter that nation's borders.
</p>
<p>
  Film scholar <strong>B. Ruby Rich</strong> attended the Sundance Film Festival, filing reports for Public Radio International's program, <i>The World,</i> and for the <i>San Francisco Bay Guardian</i>.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Santa Cruz Metro</i> ran a cover story on a UCSC conference titled "Reflections on Katrina," presented by the Humanities Division's <strong><i>Center for Cultural Studies</i></strong><i>,</i> featuring the photographs of assistant art professor <strong>Lewis Watts</strong> and quoting history of consciousness professor <strong>James Clifford</strong>.
</p>
<p>
  <strong>David Williams,</strong> professor of physics, was quoted in a profile of his former graduate student, <strong>Miguel Morales,</strong> in the magazine <i>Diverse: Issues in Higher Education.</i><br>
  <br>
  <strong>Shelby Graham</strong>, director of the Mary Porter Sesnon Art Gallery, was quoted in a <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> article about an exhibit she curated of nontraditional photography featuring artists from the Bay Area and New York.<br>
  <br>
  Professor of classics and comparative literature <strong>Mary-Kay Gamel</strong> was interviewed on <i>KZSC</i> Radio about the importance of academic humanities programs.<br>
</p>

</p>
