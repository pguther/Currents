<font color="#003399">Men's and women's soccer teams reach NCAA tournament</font>
<p>
  <font color="#000000">For the first time ever, the men's soccer team made the NCAA Division III West Regional playoffs this fall. Led by second-year head coach Paul Holocher, the Slugs finished the regular season with a 14-5-1 record, the best in school history. Claremont-Mudd-Scripps defeated the Slugs 2-1 in the first round of the playoffs.</font> <a href="http://www.GoSlugs.com/varsity/soccer/men/msoc.html">Read about the men's team.</a>
</p>
<p>
  For the second year in a row, the women's soccer team, coached by Paul Lester, earned a spot in the NCAA Division III tournament. The Slugs went 12-4-1 in regular season play and lost in the first round of the tournament to Chapman University. <a href="http://www.GoSlugs.com/varsity/soccer/women/wsoc.html">Read about the women's team.</a>
</p>

<h4>
  <font color="#003399">Workshop offers help in '<b>Uncluttering Your Desk</b>'</font>
</h4>
<p>
  Does your desk look like a disaster zone? Tired of searching for that important document? This workshop, titled Uncluttering Your Desk," will teach you how to deal with paper piles, mail, filing systems, and paper-retention issues. Find out how to streamline your work day with hands-on strategies and techniques for managing paper flow and clutter.
</p>
<ul>
  <li>Presenter: Farol Henderson, professional organizer with her own consulting business
  </li>
  <li>Date: Thursday, December 7
  </li>
  <li>Time: 8:30 a.m. - 12:30 p.m.
  </li>
  <li>Fee: $70
  </li>
</ul>
<p>
  To enroll in a workshop, e-mail workshop@cats.ucsc.edu. Please include the following information in the request: name and date of the program or workshop; employee name; employee telephone extension and e-mail address; unit and campus mailing address; employee job title; and immediate supervisor's name, phone extension, and e-mail address.
