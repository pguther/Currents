<a name="content" id="content"></a>
<p>
  Research on the SARS virus by a group of UCSC researchers led by <b>William Scott</b>, associate professor of chemistry and biochemistry, was featured in <i>Biology Digest, Science Daily</i>, and a variety of weekly newsletters, including <i>Life Science Weekly, Biotech Week, Drug Week, Science Letter, Virus Weekly</i>, and <i>Health &amp; Medicine Week</i>.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>San Jose Mercury News</i> and <i>Register Pajaronian</i> ran articles about a $1.4 million gift to UCSC--the largest single donation ever given by an alum--from UC Santa Cruz Foundation trustee <b>Kit Mura-smith</b>.
</p>
<p>
  <b>Mark Carr,</b> associate professor of ecology and evolutionary biology, was quoted in a <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> article about plans to establish a series of marine protected areas along the California coast.
</p>
<p>
  The January 2005 issue of <i>Art in America</i> magazine ran a feature about a traveling exhibition of the Ant Farm, the innovative architecture collective cofounded by <b>Chip Lord,</b> chair of the Film and Digital Media Department.
</p>
<p>
  <b>Ann Caudle</b>, director of the Science Illustration Program, was quoted in an article about science illustration in the <i>Christian Science Monitor</i>. <b>Stacey Vigallon</b>, a recent graduate of the program, was also featured in the story.
</p>
<p>
  Theater arts professor <b>Kathy Foley</b> was quoted in a <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> article about tsunami fundraising efforts in South Asia.
</p>
<p>
  Assistant director of library development <b>Liz Sandoval</b> was quoted in a <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> article about an appearance by author Jonathan Franzen that was cosponsored by Friends of the UCSC Library.
</p>
