<a name="content" id="content"></a>
<p>
  The first phase of the Ranch View Terrace project, a single-family<br>
  development for faculty and staff, will include 45 units (<a href="http://currents.ucsc.edu/04-05/05-09/ranchview.asp">see earlier Currents story</a>).
</p>
<p>
  Anyone interested in this development who is not currently on a waiting list should contact the Faculty and Staff Housing Office at (831) 458-3506 or by sending email to: <a href="mailto:facultyhousing@ucsc.edu">facultyhousing@ucsc.edu</a>.<br>
</p>
<p>
  For more information on the project, go to:<br>
  <a href="http://housing.ucsc.edu/housing/rvtbrochure.pdf">housing.ucsc.edu/housing/rvtbrochure.pdf</a><br>
</p>
