<a name="content" id="content"></a>
<p>
  The <i>San Jose Mercury News, Santa Cruz Sentinel, Watsonville Register-Pajaronian,</i> and <i>Good Times</i> all ran feature stories on UCSC's 2005 Pacific Rim Music Festival, quoting artistic director and associate music professor <b>Hi Kyung Kim</b> and music professor <b>Nicole Paiement</b>. <i>Metro Santa Cruz</i> also ran a story on the festival quoting music lecturer <b>John Sackett</b>.
</p>
<p>
  Biology graduate student <b>Robin Dunkin</b> was quoted in a story in the <i>Watsonville Register-Pajaronian</i> about recent killer whale attacks on migrating gray whales in Monterey Bay.
</p>
<p>
  American studies professor <b>Tricia Rose</b> was quoted in the <i>Chicago Sun-Times</i> about her participation in a three-day conference on feminism and hip-hop in Chicago aimed at battling hip-hop stereotypes.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Aptos Times</i> ran a story about research on wireless communication networks by <b>J. J. Garcia-Luna-Aceves</b>, professor of computer engineering.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> ran a review of Music Department lecturer <b>Mesut Ozgen</b>'s concert for the Cabrillo College Distinguished Artists Series, calling it a display of "spectacular classical guitar."
</p>
<p>
  Mathematical models of fish populations developed by <b>Marc Mangel</b>, professor of applied math and statistics, were the focus of an article in <i>SIAM News</i>, published by the Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics.
</p>
