<a name="content" id="content"></a>
<p>
  Trustees from the UC Santa Cruz Foundation, including Vice President Anu Luther, Talat and Kamil Hasan, and Kiran Malhotra, were involved in planning a major tsunami relief event, <a href="http://www.BayAreaUnites.org">Bay Area Unites</a>, that featured acclaimed author Deepak Chopra, Grammy-nominated Lisa Loeb, and a taped appearance from former President Bill Clinton.
</p>
<p>
  UCSC was also one of the sponsors of the event, which took place February 20 at the HP Pavilion in San Jose and raised <a href="http://sanjose.bizjournals.com/sanjose/stories/2005/02/21/daily3.html">more than $1.5 million</a> for relief efforts.
</p><br>
