<p>
  Economist <b>Robert Fairlie</b> was quoted in a <i>Washington Times</i> story about the digital divide, saying it appears to have fallen off the agenda of legislators. Fairlie's research was also featured on the <a href="http://www.digitaldividenetwork.org/">Digital Divide Network</a>'s web page.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Santa Cruz</i> <i>Sentinel</i> featured front-page coverage of postdoctoral researcher <b>Phil Howard's</b> survey of consumers regarding their food purchases. Howard, of the Center for Agroecology &amp; Sustainable Food Systems, has fielded calls from Public Citizen and others about the survey. His work will be written up in the March issue of <i>Sustainable Industries Journal,</i> and Howard will appear on KUSP's <i>Talk of the Bay</i> program on Wednesday, February 2.
</p>
<p>
  <b>Dan Wirls</b> of politics provided postinauguration analysis for the <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> about President Bush's second-term agenda.
</p>
