<a name="content" id="content"></a>
<p>
  Bob Laird, former director of admissions at UC Berkeley and author of the book, <i>The Case for Affirmative Action In University Admissions</i>, will give a talk at 4 p.m. Thursday, October 27, at the Merrill College Events Center.
</p>
<p>
  Laird's talk will be followed by a question-and-answer session and a book-signing.
</p>
<p>
  The event is free and open to the public. It is sponsored by the American Indian Resource Center, the Office of Admissions, and the Educational Partnership Center.
</p>
<p>
  For more information call (831) 459-2881 or e-mail: <a href="mailto:dtibbett@ucsc.edu">dtibbett@ucsc.edu</a>.
</p>
