<a name="content" id="content"></a>
<p>
  An opinion piece by biologist <b>Terrie Williams</b> on the Endangered Species Act, sea otters, and the value of wildlife ran in <i>USA Today</i>.
</p>
<p>
  The international weekly newspaper <i>India Abroad</i> published an extensive feature/profile of assistant professor of music <b>Dard Neuman</b>, holder of the Kamil and Talat Hasan Endowed Chair in Classical Indian Music, spotlighting his plans to promote Indian music at UCSC.
</p>
<p>
  A new partnership between UCSC and Los Alamos National Laboratory was covered in the <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> and <i>Salinas Californian</i>, with quotes from computer scientist <b>Darrell Long</b>.
</p>
<p>
  <b>Michael Riordan</b>, adjunct professor of physics, was quoted in a story in the <i>Newark Star-Ledger</i> about the history and future of Bell Labs.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> ran a feature story on <b>Todd Newberry</b>, professor emeritus of biology, and his new book, <i>The Ardent Birder</i>.
</p>
<p>
  Geologist <b>Gary Griggs</b> was quoted in a <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> story about his new book on the coastal geology of California, which was also featured in the <i>Monterey Herald</i>. Griggs was also quoted in a <i>Sentinel</i> story about renewed funding for the Center for Integrated Marine Technologies.
</p>
<p>
  A <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> story about the endangered Santa Cruz long-toed salamander included quotes from biology graduate student <b>Antonia D'Amore</b>.
</p>
<p>
  Physicist <b>Robert Johnson</b> was quoted in a story on <i>Space Daily</i> about the detector his group built for NASA's GLAST telescope.
</p>
<p>
  <b>James Zachos</b>, professor of Earth sciences, was featured in a story on <i>Mongabay.com</i> about an episode of extreme climate change 55 million years ago.
</p>
<p>
  A story on <i>Physorg.com</i> about research on Archaea (a type of microorganism) featured <b>Todd Lowe</b>, assistant professor of biomolecular engineering, and <b>Chad Saltikov</b>, assistant professor of environmental toxicology.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> praised the "unique and unexpected pleasures" of the student productions presented by the Theater Arts Department in an article about a recent UCSC theater performance.
</p>
<p>
  <i>City on a Hill</i> interviewed professor of English and comparative literature <b>Murray Baumgarten</b> in a story about the $195,452 grant he received from the National Endowment for the Humanities to fund a summer institute for college and university teachers in Italy.<br>
</p>
