<a name="content" id="content"></a>
<p>
  <b>Joe's Pizza and Subs</b> will be open from 11 a.m. to 5 p.m., Monday through Friday.
</p>
<p>
  <b>Stevenson Coffee House</b> will be open from 8 a.m. to 3 p.m., Monday through Thursday, and 8 a.m. to 2 p.m. on Friday.
</p>
<p>
  <b>Kresge Cafe</b> will be open from 11 a.m. to 2:30 p.m., Monday through Friday.
</p>
<p>
  The <b>University Center</b> restaurant will be open one day a week for lunch beginning the week of June 20 when University Dining Services takes over for Sodexho. A buffet lunch will be served on Wednesdays from 11:30 a.m. to 2 p.m. at a cost of $9.95, including beverage.
</p>
<p>
  Cafes at Cowell, Merrill, Porter, Oakes, and Eight will be closed for the summer.
</p>
