<a name="content" id="content"></a>
<p>
  Research by glacier expert <b>Slawek Tulaczyk</b> and graduate student <b>Ian Howat</b> on melting glaciers in Greenland was reported in the <i>Independent</i> (London), <i>Canberra Times</i>, Discovery Channel News, <i>ZNet.com</i>, and <i>Livescience.com</i>.
</p>
<p>
  Research professor <b>Bill Domhoff</b> penned a letter that appeared in the <i>New York Times</i> about government policies to fight inflation and promote high employment, saying monetary policy is also a political mechanism used to control the workforce.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>San Jose Mercury News, Santa Cruz Sentinel,</i> and <i>Monterey Herald</i> ran stories about <b>Jack Baskin</b> being chosen for the Silicon Valley Engineering Hall of Fame, with quotes from <b>Steve Kang</b>, dean of the Baskin School of Engineering.
</p>
<p>
  Professor emerita of American studies <b>Judy Yung</b> was featured in a <i>Contra Costa Times</i> story about preserving stories of immigration on Angel Island with the help of a $15 million grant authorized by Congress in November.
</p>
<p>
  Mentor programs like those developed by the New Teacher Center were featured in a Gannett News Service article about the travails of new teachers. NTC director <b>Ellen Moir</b> was quoted.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>San Francisco Chronicle</i> ran a story about research by <b>Roberto Manduchi</b>, assistant professor of computer engineering, to develop new technologies for the blind.
</p>
<p>
  Economist <b>Rob Fairlie</b>'s research on the digital divide was cited in a <i>Milwaukee Journal Sentinel</i> story about 100 families who received computers donated for home use.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> wrote about a new book by history professor <b>Bruce Levine</b>, titled <i>Confederate Emancipation: Southern Plans to Free and Arm Slaves During the Civil War</i>.
</p>
<p>
  A story about the proposed removal of the bald eagle from the endangered species list in the <i>Sacramento Bee</i> and other Scripps-Howard newspapers included a quote from <b>Brian Walton</b>, coordinator of the Santa Cruz Predatory Bird Research Group.
</p>
<p>
  History of consciousness professor <b>Angela Davis</b> was quoted in the <i>Penn State Digital Collegian</i> about an appearance at Penn State where she contended that racism is inherent in capital punishment.<br>
  <br>
  Music lecturer <b>William Coulter</b> was quoted in a <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> article about a holiday show, titled Parcel From America, that he developed with Irish writer/storyteller Tomaseen Foley.<br>
</p>
