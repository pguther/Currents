<p>
  The Women at Work Retreat is still accepting applications for the upcoming April 21-22 retreat in San Juan Bautista.
</p>

  This year's theme is the Art of Adapting to Change: Juggling Tradition and Innovation.<br>
</p>
<p>
  Space is limited; applications will be accepted until registration is filled. For more information or an application, contact Dale Whyte at (831) 459-5652, <a href="mailto:whyte@ucsc.edu">whyte@ucsc.edu</a>, or Laura McCann at (831) 459-2228, <a href="mailto:llmccann@ucsc.edu">llmccann@ucsc.edu</a>.
