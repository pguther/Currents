<p>
  E. Melanie DuPuis, associate professor of sociology, received the 2005 Frederick H. Buttel Outstanding Scholarship Award from the Rural Sociological Society in recognition of her book, <i>Nature's Perfect Food</i>.
</p>
<p>
  The award was presented during the association's annual meeting in Tampa in August.
