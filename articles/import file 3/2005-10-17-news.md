<a name="content" id="content"></a>
<p>
  Economist <b>Rob Fairlie</b>'s work with the Kauffman Foundation on the rate of U.S. entrepreneurship received widespread media coverage, including stories that appeared in the <i>Chicago Tribune</i>, <i>Seattle Times, St. Louis Post-Dispatch</i>, and <i>Contra Costa Times</i>. Fairlie's research on the digital divide was covered by <i>National Journal'</i>s "Technology Daily."
</p>
<p>
  Biologist <b>Jim Estes</b> was quoted in a <i>San Jose Mercury News</i> story about a recent federal decision changing the management of the California sea otter population.
</p>
<p>
  Literature professor and director of the UCSC Institute for Advanced Feminist Research <b>Helene Moglen</b> was quoted in two <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> articles about the institute's conference on body modification. Art professor <b>Joyce Brodsky</b> and Sesnon Gallery director <b>Shelby Graham</b> were also quoted in the <i>Sentinel</i>. The <i>Good Times</i> and <i>City on the Hill Press</i> also ran features on the conference.
</p>
<p>
  Sociology instructor <b>Mike Males</b> was quoted widely regarding a study about the frequency of fatal drug overdoses among the middle-aged. The story, which first appeared in the <i>Los Angeles Times</i>, was picked up as far away as Korea.
</p>
<p>
  Biologist <b>Mark Carr</b> was quoted in a <i>Monterey Herald</i> story about the possibility of developing an oil refinery on the site of the former Fort Ord.
</p>
<p>
  UCSC Farm manager <b>Jim Leap</b> was contacted by <i>Time</i> magazine for a farm-to-cafeteria story focused on campus farms that supply college cafeterias.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> noted that film and digital media professor <b>Eli Hollander</b> and music professor <b>Linda Burman-Hall</b> were screening <i>Kawitan</i>, their documentary film about natives of South Bali, at the Pacific Rim Film Festival.
</p>
<p>
  <b>John Pearse</b>, professor emeritus of ecology and evolutionary biology, was quoted in a story about marine biologist Edward F. Ricketts in the <i>Monterey Coast Weekly</i>.
</p>
