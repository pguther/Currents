<br>
<p>
  The Financial Administrative Services and Transactions (FAST) office--the new organization responsible for making vendor and reimbursement payments--begins operations today, October 3, in the 2300 Delaware building.
</p>
<p>
  FAST staff will process all purchase order-related invoices and direct payments, which include travel reimbursements and honoraria payments. A small satellite campus FAST office, located in Kerr Hall 165, also opens today to provide in-person service from 9 a.m. to 3 p.m., Monday through Friday. For more information, visit the <a href="http://www.ucsc.edu/finaff/FAST">FAST office web site</a> or call the FAST hotline at (831) 459-4488.
</p>
