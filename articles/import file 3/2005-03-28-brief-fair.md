<p>
  The Last Chance Job and Internship Fair will be held from noon to 3 p.m. April 5 at the Stevenson Event Center. The job fair is open to all registered students, and a student ID will be required to enter.
</p>
<p>
  Participants are advised to bring several copies of their resumes. A complete listing of participating employers is available online at <a href="http://www2.ucsc.edu/careers/events/lcfair.html">www2.ucsc.edu/careers/events/lcfair.html</a>
</p>
<p>
  Students may have their resumes checked during <a href="http://www2.ucsc.edu/careers/student/advising.html">Drop-In Advising</a> and may find out more by reading <a href="http://www2.ucsc.edu/careers/events/fair_tips.html">Tips to Get the Most Out of Job Fairs</a>.
</p><br>
