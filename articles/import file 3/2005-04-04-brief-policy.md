<p>
  UC Provost and Senior Vice President M.R.C. Greenwood and UC Senior Vice President Joseph Mullinix have announced a proposed update to the UC Electronic Communications Policy originally issued in November 2000. They have asked for comments on or before April 15.
</p>
<p>
  A marked up copy of the policy, reflecting changes and other background information, can be found at <a href="http://www.ucop.edu/irc/policy/ecpupdate/">www.ucop.edu/irc/policy/ecpupdate/</a>.
</p>
<p>
  Letter from Larry Merkley, vice provost for information technology: <a href="http://security.ucsc.edu/policies/ECP/ElectronicCommunicationsPolicyUpdate.pdf">security.ucsc.edu/policies/ECP/ElectronicCommunicationsPolicyUpdate.pdf</a>
</p>
