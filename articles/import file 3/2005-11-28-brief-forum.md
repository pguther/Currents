<a name="content" id="content"></a>
<p>
  Campus staff are invited to a transportation and parking forum with Wes Scott, director of UCSC Transportation and Parking Services. The forum will be held in Room 152 of the Baskin Engineering Building on Wednesday, November 30, from 12:30 to 1:40 p.m.
</p>
<p>
  Scott will provide an overview of current and future campus transportation and parking strategies. There will be plenty of time for questions after the presentation.
</p>
<p>
  Managers and supervisors are encouraged to provide release time to employees so they may attend the event.
</p>
