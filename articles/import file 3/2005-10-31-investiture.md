<p>
  Chancellor Denice Denton joined the celebration of Dia de los Muertos on October 25 at the Museum of Art &amp; History in Santa Cruz, one of several public events leading up to her investiture on Friday, November 4, as UCSC's ninth chancellor.
</p>
<p>
