<a name="content" id="content"></a>
<p>
  The <i>New York Times</i> quoted literature professor <strong>Richard Terdiman</strong> in an article about a new <a href="http://www.universityofcalifornia.edu/news/2005/oct03.html">partnership between UC Libraries and technology companies</a> to provide free public access to digitized books.
</p>
<p>
  Research on dark matter in elliptical galaxies by physicist <strong>Joel Primack,</strong> visiting researcher <strong>Avishai Dekel</strong>, and their collaborators was reported in <i>Science Now, Space Daily,</i> <i>PhysOrg.com</i> and <i>Globes Online.</i>
</p>
<p>
  New Teacher Center director <strong>Ellen Moir's</strong> recent national education award was covered by the <i>Chronicle of Higher Education</i> and the <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i>. The <i>Sentinel</i> also published an article about <strong>Faye Crosby</strong>, who recently received a top award in social psychology.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>San Jose Mercury News</i> announced that professor of linguistics <strong>Geoffrey Pullum</strong> was named one of 51 Radcliffe Institute fellows for 2005-06.
</p>
<p>
  Economist <strong>Rob Fairlie's</strong> research on the digital divide was covered by <i>National Journal's Technology Daily</i>.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> noted the efforts of theater arts lecturer <strong>Don Williams</strong> to include children in the Santa Cruz African American community for a Kids on Broadway production of <i>Ragtime</i>.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Olympian</i> (Olympia, Washington) ran a story about marine scientist <strong>Gregory Rau</strong>'s research on technology to control carbon dioxide emissions from power plants.
</p>
<p>
  Sociologist <strong>Marcia Millman</strong> was interviewed by the <i>Chicago Sun-Times</i> for an article about sisters, the topic of Millman's most recent book.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> and <i>Aptos Times</i> reported on <strong>David Haussler,</strong> professor of biomolecular engineering, receiving the Dickson Prize in Science.
</p>
<p>
  In psychology, <strong>Bruce Bridgeman</strong> appeared on KZSC Radio's <i>The Sound of Young America,</i> discussing his research on the Mystery Spot.
</p>
<p>
  An excerpt from physicist <strong>Bruce Schumm</strong>'s book <i>Deep Down Things</i> was published on <i>the Physics Central web site.</i>
</p>
