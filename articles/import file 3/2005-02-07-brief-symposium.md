<p>
  Faculty and staff from a variety of disciplines will offer ideas for engaging students at the first annual Teaching and Learning Symposium on Thursday, February 17, from 1 to 5 p.m. at the University Center.
</p>
<p>
  The event, titled "Engaging Students: Challenges &amp; Solutions for Small &amp; Large Classes," will include faculty presentations, instructional technology demonstrations, and exhibits. The afternoon will conclude with a panel discussion, titled "Technology, Student Engagement, and Learning: Are we going in the right direction?" Faculty are invited to attend all or part of the event.<br>
</p>
<p>
  The symposium, formerly known as the Convocation on Teaching, is presented by the Committee on Teaching, the Center for Teaching Excellence, Media Services, and the Information Technology Services Division.
</p>
<p>
  For a list of presentations and to register, go to the <a href="http://ic.ucsc.edu/CTE/teachsymp_reg.html">Center for Teaching Excellence</a> web site or contact the center at (831) 459-5091.
</p><br>
