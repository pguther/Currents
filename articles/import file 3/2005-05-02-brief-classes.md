<p>
  The Recreation Department is offering the following classes. Call the Recreation Office to register at (831) 459-2806 or stop by the office, located at the East Field House.
</p>
<p>
  <b>East Indian Cooking<br></b>Thursday, May 5, from 4 to 7 p.m<b><br></b>Village kitchen<br>
  $32, includes instruction and full dinner<br>
  Let Chef Jozseph Schultz, formerly of India Joze Restaurant, show you the techniques to whip up East Indian feasts for all occasions. Appetizers, relishes/salsas/chutneys, fresh breads, curries, rice dishes, and desserts will all part of the menu. Experienced cooks and neophytes alike are enthusiastic about this blend of culinary, anthropological, and nutritional tips. Vegetarians and carnivores alike are welcome. Participants will help with clean up.
</p>
<p>
  <b>Making your Own Body and Bath Product</b>s<br>
  Wednesday, May 4, from 6:30 - 9:30 p.m.<br>
  Women's Center<br>
  $25 UCSC students/$30 general<br>
  In this workshop, instructor Laurie Mount will teach you how to make your own body scrubs, lip balm, and aromatherapy bath salts. Scrubs are used in the shower as a weekly exfoliant, removing old skin, stimulating surface circulation, and nourishing skin. The class will cover the different properties of the ingredients that are used. All materials provided.<br>
  <br>
</p>
