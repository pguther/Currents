<h4>
  <font color="#993300"><br></font><font color="#003399">Slug Run draws 400 participants</font>
</h4>Runners from as far away as Nevada, and ranging in age from two to 83, came to campus April 1 for the April Fools' Slug Run 2000. The nearly 400 runners participated in one of three events: a kids' 1K, a 5K, and a 10K. (<a href="http://members.aol.com/racetimer/ucsc00.html">Runners names and times</a>) The run was a joint effort between the Santa Cruz Track Club and the UCSC Running Club, headed by writing lecturer Jeff Arnett, with support from OPERS.
<h4>
  <font color="#003399">UCSC hosting women's water polo championships</font>
</h4><font color="#000000">The collegiate women's water polo national championships will be held this year at UCSC May 5-7. Twenty games are scheduled for the tournament (eight on Friday, eight on Saturday, four on Sunday). Seed pool play on Friday and Saturday will determine championship pairings for Saturday afternoon and Sunday. All games will take place at the East Field House pool; tickets are $1 per day for students; $4 per day for adults; $10 for an adult tournament pass; free for children 12 and under. For more information, go to</font> <a href="http://www.goslugs.com/">GoSlug.com</a><font color="#000000">.</font>
<h4>
  <font color="#003399">Lecture Series at Seymour Center continues</font>
</h4>
<p>
  <img align="right" alt="drawing of a whale" border="0" height="52" src="../art/whale.200.gif" width="200"> <font color="#000000">The</font> <a href="http://www2.ucsc.edu/seymourcenter/calendar.html">Inaugural Lecture Series</a> <font color="#000000">at the Seymour Marine Discovery Center continues on April 18 with talks by assistant research biologist Baldo Marinovic on "El Nino, Whales, and Krill, Not the Story You Might Expect" and by assistant professor of ocean sciences Raphael Kudela on "Phytoplankton Dynamics--The Grass of the Sea." There will be one more evening of lectures on April 25. All the lectures take place at the Seymour Center from 7 to 9 p.m. Tickets are $6/evening or $20/series for general admission; $5/evening or $15/series for Friends of Long Marine Lab members. For tickets, call (831) 459-3799. For more information, call (831) 459-5548.</font>

<h4>
  <font color="#003399">Campus will observe Veterans Day, November 11</font>
</h4>
<p>
  <font color="#000000">In February President Atkinson announced that the University of California will honor all veterans by designating Veterans Day, November 11, as one of its official holidays beginning in fall quarter 2000. In order to incorporate the holiday and meet the minimum days of instruction, classes at UCSC will begin on September 20, rather than the previously scheduled September 21.</font>
</p>
