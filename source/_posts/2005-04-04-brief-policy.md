UC Provost and Senior Vice President M.R.C. Greenwood and UC Senior Vice President Joseph Mullinix have announced a proposed update to the UC Electronic Communications Policy originally issued in November 2000. They have asked for comments on or before April 15.

A marked up copy of the policy, reflecting changes and other background information, can be found at [www.ucop.edu/irc/policy/ecpupdate/][1].

Letter from Larry Merkley, vice provost for information technology: [security.ucsc.edu/policies/ECP/ElectronicCommunicationsPolicyUpdate.pdf][2]
