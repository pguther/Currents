**Bill Domhoff**, research professor of psychology and sociology, featured prominently in a major story in _U.S. News & World Report_ about dreams. The article referred to Domhoff's database of 16,000 dreams that facilitates his work.

**Ellen Moir,** director of the New Teacher Center, was featured in an article in the _Times Educational Supplement_ about her visit to Scotland, where she was invited to brief leaders of education about the center's cutting-edge work in teacher training and leadership.

In a Sunday "Datebook" article about summer Shakespeare festivals. the _San Francisco Chronicle_ noted that **Shakespeare Santa Cruz** will be presenting _King Lear._

The _Santa Cruz Sentinel_ announced a gift of $75,000 from a consortium of donors to fund Hindi/Urdu language classes at UCSC, initiated by Silicon Valley entrepreneurs Kamil and Talat Hasan.
