<p>
  <b>Bill Domhoff</b>, research professor of psychology and sociology, featured prominently in a major story in <i>U.S. News &amp; World Report</i> about dreams. The article referred to Domhoff's database of 16,000 dreams that facilitates his work.
</p>
<p>
  <b>Ellen Moir,</b> director of the New Teacher Center, was featured in an article in the <i>Times Educational Supplement</i> about her visit to Scotland, where she was invited to brief leaders of education about the center's cutting-edge work in teacher training and leadership.
</p>
<p>
  In a Sunday "Datebook" article about summer Shakespeare festivals. the <i>San Francisco Chronicle</i> noted that <b>Shakespeare Santa Cruz</b> will be presenting <i>King Lear.</i>
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> announced a gift of $75,000 from a consortium of donors to fund Hindi/Urdu language classes at UCSC, initiated by Silicon Valley entrepreneurs Kamil and Talat Hasan.
</p>
