<p class="pagehead">
  UCSC in the News
</p>
<p>
  Astronomer <b>Garth Illingworth</b> was quoted in stories about the Hubble Ultra Deep Field, a spectacular new image from the Hubble Space Telescope, in <i>Science</i> magazine and on the web site <i>Space.com.</i>
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Chronicle of Higher Education</i> quoted history professor <b>Peter Kenez</b> in a story about the scholarly debate during the cold war about what fueled the survival of the Soviet regime.<br>
</p>
<p>
  History of consciousness professor <b>Angela Davis</b> was featured in a <i>Binghamton Press &amp; Sun-Bulletin</i> article about a speech she gave at Binghamton University urging students to make a difference.<br>
</p>
<p>
  An article in the <i>Los Angeles Times</i> about efforts to save the endangered Channel Islands foxes included a quote from <b>Brian Latta,</b> a biologist with the Predatory Bird Research Group, who is trying to remove from the island the golden eagles that have been preying on the foxes.<br>
</p>
<p>
  Women's studies professor <b>Bettina Aptheker</b> contributed two reviews of books about women in Afghanistan to the <i>Women's Review of Books.</i><br>
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Dartmouth Staff</i> noted that American studies professor and chair <b>Tricia Rose</b> would deliver an address at a Northeast conference titled "Hip Hop Identities and Poetic Race Relations."<br>
</p>
<p>
  Arboretum director <b>Dan Harder</b> and curator <b>Melinda Johnson</b> were quoted in a <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> story about gardening in a Mediterranean climate.
</p>
<p>
  Music lecturer <b>Mesut Ozgen</b> was quoted in a <i>Register-Pajaronian</i> article about his multimedia project "New Dimensions in Classical Guitar."<br>
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> gave a rave review to the UCSC theater production <i>Ruy Blas,</i> praising the "incomparable imagination" of its director, theater arts professor and department chair <b>Danny Scheie.</b>
</p>
