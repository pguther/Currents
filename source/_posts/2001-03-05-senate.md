The February 21 meeting of UCSC's Academic Senate, which ended before the agenda could be completed, will resume on Thursday, March 8.  
  
The meeting will begin at 3 p.m. in the Mainstage of the Theater Arts Center.  
  
Senators are asked to bring their "call" from the February 21 meeting. Those needing a new copy may get one via the following web site: <http://senate.ucsc.edu/call/>.
