<h4>
  <font color="#003399">Shakespeare Santa Cruz extends its summer festival season; special discounts available for faculty and staff</font>
</h4>
<p>
  <img align="right" border="0" height="171" src="../art/shakespeare.sc.150.jpg" width="150" alt="">Due to a high demand for tickets, <a href="http://www.shakespearesantacruz.org">Shakespeare Santa Cruz</a> (SSC) has extended its summer festival season through Sunday, September 3, adding nine performances. These are:
</p>
<ul>
  <li>
    <i>Cymbeline:</i> Wednesday, August 30; Friday, September 1; and Saturday, September 2--all at 8 p.m.
  </li>
  <li>
    <i>Kean:</i> Wednesday, August 30; Friday, September 1; and Saturday, September 2--all at 8 p.m.
  </li>
  <li>
    <i>Love's Labour's Lost:</i> Thursday, August 31, 8 p.m.; Saturday, September 2, 2 p.m.; and Sunday, September 3, 2 p.m.
  </li>
</ul>
<p>
  SSC also offers a discount on tickets to UCSC staff and faculty. Regular single-ticket prices range from $24 to $32, but are available to UCSC employees for $21. The special ticket prices are available for all shows between Sunday evening and Friday evening but cannot be used for Saturday or Sunday matinees. For tickets, call (831) 459-2159.
</p>
<p>
  Groups of 15 or more can take advantage of further discounts. Prices and details can be found at the SSC <a href="http://www.shakespearesantacruz.org/summer00/groupds.shtml">Group Sales Page</a>. For more information, call Terri Fette at (831) 457-8549.
</p>
