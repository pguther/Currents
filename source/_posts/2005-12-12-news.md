<p>
  Physicist and author <b>Michael Riordan</b> has an opinion piece in the December issue of the <i>Harvard Business Review</i> about how innovation flourished at AT&amp;T before the company was broken up. Riordan also published an article in <i>IEEE Spectrum</i> on "How Europe Missed the Transistor," about a second invention of the transistor in Paris that eventually fizzled out commercially.
</p>
<p>
  Physicist <b>Robert Johnson</b> was quoted in a story in the <i>Aptos Times</i> about his team's work on the GLAST space telescope project. The <i>Aptos Times</i> also ran a story about engineers <b>J. J. Garcia-Luna-Aceves</b> and <b>Darrell Long</b>, who were both named Fellows of the Institute of Electrical and Electronic Engineers.
</p>
<p>
  <i>Metro Santa Cruz</i> ran a story on <i>COSMOGONIE INTIME</i>, a newly published, limited-edition, collaborative book of art on exhibit at <b>McHenry Library's Special Collections</b>, featuring French poet Yves Peyre, visual artist Ray Rice, and award-winning book artist and UCSC alumna <b>Felicia Rice</b>.<br>
</p>
