<p>
  Professor of music and dean of arts Edward Houghton is the author of"Ockeghemis Scribes, Then and Now," an article published recently in <i>Johannes Ockeghem, Actes du XLe Colloque Internationale diEtudes Humanistes.</i> The <i>Actes</i> cover the proceedings of a week-long conference held in Tours, France, in February, 1997, that was sponsored by the Centre diEtudes Superieures de la Renaissance to mark the 500th anniversary of the composeris death.

<h4>
  <font color="#003399">Melanie Mayer</font>
</h4>
<p>
  Melanie Mayer's latest book on women's history, <i>Staking her Claim,</i> has just been released by <a href="http://www.ohiou.edu/~oupress/">Ohio University Press</a>. It is the biography of Belinda<br>
  Mulrooney, who was a very successful entrepreneur in the Klondike and Alaskan gold rush era at the turn of the last century. Mayer is professor emerita of psychology.
