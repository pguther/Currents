<p class="pagehead">
  UCSC in the News
</p>
<p>
  Chemistry professors <b>Bakthan Singaram</b> and <b>Todd Wipke</b> were quoted in news stories about an optical glucose sensor developed in Singaram's lab. Stories appeared in the <i>Washington Times,</i> U.P.I. news wire, <i>Medical News Today, Medical News U.K.,</i> <i>WebIndia.com,</i> and the <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel.</i>
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> ran a lengthy interview with Chancellor <b>M.R.C. Greenwood</b> covering the successes and challenges of her eight years at UCSC and her expectations for her new position with UC President Robert Dynes.
</p>
<p>
  American studies professor and department chair <b>Tricia Rose</b> was quoted in the <i>Chicago Tribune</i> and <i>Denver Post</i> about the influence of pop star Britney Spears.<br>
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> noted that music lecturer <b>Robert Klevan</b> was recently named as jazz education director for the Monterey Jazz Festival.<br>
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Register-Pajaronian</i> published a lengthy review of music lecturer <b>Mesut Ozgen</b>'s multimedia concert featuring assistant film and digital media professor <b>Gustavo Vazquez,</b> music lecturer <b>Peter Elsea,</b> and assistant theater arts professor <b>David Cuthbert,</b> at the Mello Center in Watsonville.
</p>
