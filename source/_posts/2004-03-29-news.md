UCSC in the News

Chemistry professors **Bakthan Singaram** and **Todd Wipke** were quoted in news stories about an optical glucose sensor developed in Singaram's lab. Stories appeared in the _Washington Times,_ U.P.I. news wire, _Medical News Today, Medical News U.K.,_ _WebIndia.com, _and the_ Santa Cruz Sentinel._

The _Santa Cruz Sentinel_ ran a lengthy interview with Chancellor **M.R.C. Greenwood** covering the successes and challenges of her eight years at UCSC and her expectations for her new position with UC President Robert Dynes.

American studies professor and department chair **Tricia Rose** was quoted in the _Chicago Tribune_ and _Denver Post_ about the influence of pop star Britney Spears.   

The _Santa Cruz Sentinel_ noted that music lecturer** Robert Klevan** was recently named as jazz education director for the Monterey Jazz Festival.  

The _Register-Pajaronian_ published a lengthy review of music lecturer **Mesut Ozgen**'s multimedia concert featuring assistant film and digital media professor** Gustavo Vazquez,** music lecturer **Peter Elsea,** and assistant theater arts professor **David Cuthbert,** at the Mello Center in Watsonville.
