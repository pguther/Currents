The chancellor's second brown-bag forum with staff will be on Monday, May 23, from 12:30 to 1:40 p.m. in Classroom Unit I.

Chancellor Denton will provide an update on campus issues and activities, and there will be an opportunity for questions. Refreshments will be provided.
