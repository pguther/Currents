In the coming months, the UC systemwide Long-Range Guidance Team will be holding a series of web chats with UC faculty, staff, alumni, students, and other friends. The purpose is to provide updates on the team's progress, but also to seek comments from these critical UC stakeholders about the issues they see facing the university in the year 2025.

Faculty, Wednesday, Nov. 2, 4-5 p.m.  
Alumni, Wednesday, Dec. 7, 4-5 p.m.  
Staff, Wednesday, Jan. 11, 4-5 p.m.  
Students, Wednesday, Feb. 22, 4-5 p.m.

The sessions will feature co-chairs Provost and Senior Vice President M.R.C. Greenwood and Senior Vice President Bruce Darling, as well as other team members.  

For more information, visit the [UC 2025][1] web site.
