<p>
  The Academic Senate <a href="http://senate.ucsc.edu/cep/">Committee on Educational Policy</a> is hosting a February 10 forum for faculty, staff, and students on student retention and graduation rates.
</p>
<p>
  Presentations about UCSC's past and current performance will be followed by general discussion at the forum, from 3:30 to 5 p.m. at the Stevenson Event Center.
</p>
<p>
  Comments and questions at the forum are intended to inform ongoing consideration by Senate committees of recommendations for improving retention.<br>
