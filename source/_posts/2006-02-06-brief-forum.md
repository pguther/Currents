The Academic Senate [Committee on Educational Policy][1] is hosting a February 10 forum for faculty, staff, and students on student retention and graduation rates.

Presentations about UCSC's past and current performance will be followed by general discussion at the forum, from 3:30 to 5 p.m. at the Stevenson Event Center.

Comments and questions at the forum are intended to inform ongoing consideration by Senate committees of recommendations for improving retention.
