<p class="sectionhead">
  <img align="right" height="267" src="../art/extension_cover.200.jpg" width="200" alt=""><a name="cover" id="cover"></a><span class="sectionhead">UCSC Extension wins award for brochure cover</span>
</p>
<p>
  UCSC Extension received the Best Brochure Cover Award for Continuing Education from <a href="http://www.lern.org/">Learning Resources Network</a> (LERN) for its Winter 2003 catalog.
</p>
<p>
  The cover was designed by Joan Boffa-Dentone, art director at UCSC Extension's Marketing Department.
</p>
<p>
  LERN credited Extension for a "professional and high-tech theme, with courses on robotics and programming on the cover, mixes well with the graphic design of this cover."
</p>
<p>
  LERN is an international association for providers of continuing education and lifelong learning.<br>

<p class="sectionhead">
  <a name="atkinson" id="atkinson"></a>President Atkinson wins NSF award for lifetime contributions
</p>
<p>
  University of California President Richard C. Atkinson has been named recipient of the National Science Foundation's 2003 Vannevar Bush award for lifetime contributions to the nation in science and technology.<br>
</p>
<p>
  Atkinson will receive the award from the National Science Board at a May 21 awards dinner at the Department of State in Washington, D.C. He is the 25th recipient of the award since its inception in 1980.<br>
</p>
<p>
  The Bush award recognizes the recipient's public service contributions in addition to calling attention to the important role science and technology play in improving our way of life.<br>
</p>
