Psychology's** Margarita Azmitia** figured prominently in a _Santa Cruz County Sentinel _article about the challenges of the "'tween" years, when children long to be grown up and their parents are wringing their hands and bracing for the teen years.  
  
**Donald Saposnek**, also of psychology, was quoted in the _San Francisco Examiner's_ Sunday magazine's cover story about the slaying of Nina Lieberman-Donney. Saposnek, who was child custody mediator in the case, was interviewed regarding his testimony during the sentencing hearing.
