The Staff Advisory Board will host the Spring Brown-bag with Chancellor Denton on Tuesday, May 23, from 12:30 to 1:40 p.m. in the Stevenson Event Center.

Chancellor Denton will present an update on campus issues and activities and provide an opportunity for questions. Refreshments will be provided.

Managers and supervisors are encouraged to provide release time to employees to attend this event, based on operational considerations.
