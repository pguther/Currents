Film scholar **B. Ruby Rich** of community studies was quoted in the _Life_ magazine cover story about actress Amy Adams. Rich was a juror at the annual Sundance Film Festival.

The _Santa Cruz Sentinel_ ran a front-page story about research on sea lions and other marine mammals led by **Daniel Costa**, professor of ecology and evolutionary biology.

Astrophysicist **Stan Woosley** was quoted in stories about a recently detected gamma-ray burst in the _Baltimore Sun, Monterey County Herald, Biloxi Sun Herald, Bradenton Herald_, and _Albany Times Union_.

The _San Jose Mercury News_ announced the summer lineup for **Shakespeare Santa Cruz**'s 25th anniversary summer season amid the redwood groves.

The _Mid-County Post_ ran a story about research on global warming by **James Zachos**, professor of Earth sciences.

Findings on the formation of protein fibrils associated with Parkinson's disease from biochemist **Anthony Fink**'s lab were reported in _Biotech Week, Life Science Weekly, Science Letter, Proteomics Weekly_, and other newsletters.

Virginia's _ Roanoke Times_ noted that history of consciousness professor **Angela Davis** would be one of several major speakers to appear at Virginia Tech this year in a lecture series that also includes former presidential adviser and ABC News correspondent George Stephanopoulos and the Rev. Jesse Jackson.

The_ Santa Cruz Sentinel_ quoted Shakespeare Santa Cruz director and theater arts professor **Paul Whitworth** in an article about the festival's 2006 summer lineup.

_College and Research Libraries News_ announced that **Virginia Steel** was appointed as the new University Librarian at UCSC.
