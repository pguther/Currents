The _Santa Cruz Sentinel_ ran a front-page story about _Then & Now: Santa Cruz Coast_, a new book by geologist **Gary Griggs** and Deepika Shrestha Ross.

Biology graduate student **Shaye Wolf** is featured in an article in _Audubon_ magazine, along with biologists **Donald Croll** and **Bernie Tershy**. The story focuses on their efforts to protect island-nesting seabirds from rats and other nonnative predators.
