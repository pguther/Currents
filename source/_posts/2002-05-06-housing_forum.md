<p>
  Faculty-staff housing is the subject of an open forum from noon to 1:45 p.m. on Monday, May 20, at the Media Theater, Room M110. All faculty and staff members are invited to attend and offer their input.<br>
  <br>
  The Campus Welfare Committee will solicit input on faculty-staff housing principles and policies development.<br>
  <br>
  John Simpson, campus provost and executive vice chancellor, has directed the committee to review current housing policy.<br>
