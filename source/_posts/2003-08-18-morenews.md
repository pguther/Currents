More News

Michael Moore to speak on October 19

![Michael Moore][1]Academy and Emmy Award-winning documentary filmmaker and best-selling author Michael Moore has been added to the UCSC Arts & Lectures schedule.

Moore will speak on Sunday, October 19, at 7 p.m. at Santa Cruz Civic Auditorium, 307 Church St., Santa Cruz.  

Subscription tickets will not be available for this special presentation. Tickets for this event are available to A&L members during the early ticketing period from August 1-31 through the UCSC Ticket Office.  

Starting September 2, tickets go on sale to the general public through the Santa Cruz Civic Auditorium Box Office, (831) 420-5260, or Ticketmaster, (408) 998-8497. A&L membership information is available [online.][2]

Pastry chef teaching cooking class

Local pastry chef Gardner Lund will be featured in the second in a series of family cooking events in the Life Lab garden classroom from 10 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. on Sunday, August 24. The program is in collaboration with the UCSC Center for Agroecology & Sustainable Food Systems.

The cost is $25 for adults, $10 for children. To make reservations, call (831) 459-2001 or e-mail [lifelab@lifelab.org.][3]
