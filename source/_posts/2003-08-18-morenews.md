  <a name="moore" id="moore"></a>Michael Moore to speak on October 19
</p>
<p>
  <img align="right" alt="Michael Moore" height="228" src="../art/moore.jpg" width="200">Academy and Emmy Award-winning documentary filmmaker and best-selling author Michael Moore has been added to the UCSC Arts &amp; Lectures schedule.
</p>
<p>
  Moore will speak on Sunday, October 19, at 7 p.m. at Santa Cruz Civic Auditorium, 307 Church St., Santa Cruz.<br>
</p>
<p>
  Subscription tickets will not be available for this special presentation. Tickets for this event are available to A&amp;L members during the early ticketing period from August 1-31 through the UCSC Ticket Office.<br>
</p>
<p>
  Starting September 2, tickets go on sale to the general public through the Santa Cruz Civic Auditorium Box Office, (831) 420-5260, or Ticketmaster, (408) 998-8497. A&amp;L membership information is available <a href="http://events.ucsc.edu/artslecs/Membership.html">online.</a><br>

  <a name="pastry" id="pastry"></a><span class="sectionhead">Pastry chef teaching cooking class</span>
</p>
<p>
  Local pastry chef Gardner Lund will be featured in the second in a series of family cooking events in the Life Lab garden classroom from 10 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. on Sunday, August 24. The program is in collaboration with the UCSC Center for Agroecology &amp; Sustainable Food Systems.
</p>
<p>
  The cost is $25 for adults, $10 for children. To make reservations, call (831) 459-2001 or e-mail <a href="mailto:lifelab@lifelab.org">lifelab@lifelab.org.</a><br>
