  <b>Ravi Rajan</b> of environmental studies was interviewed by KCBS on Subcontinental politics following the coup in Pakistan on October 13. The interview was played on KCBS news bulletins during the day .<br>
  <br>
  <b>Gary Griggs</b>, director of the Institute of Marine Sciences, wrote an editorial on the subject of stewardship of the earth for the <i>San Jose Mercury News</i>.<br>
  <br>
