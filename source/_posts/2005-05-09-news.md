The media were on hand when research associates **Glenn Stewart** and **Brian Latta** banded four peregrine falcon chicks in a nest on the PG&E building in San Francisco. Coverage included the _San Francisco Chronicle, Contra Costa Times, Santa Cruz Sentinel_, Associated Press, and KPIX TV.

Bombay's _Indian Express_ quoted associate professor of history **Dilip Basu** in an article about his role in arranging a screening of Indian director Satyajit Ray's film _Pather Panchali_ at the 2005 Cannes Film Festival.

The _Santa Cruz Sentinel_ ran a story about whether Santa Cruz County beaches are shrinking, with quotes from geologists **Gerald Weber** and **Gary Griggs**.

Geophysicist **Steven Ward** was quoted in a story about tsunami warning systems in _American Scientist_ magazine.

The _San Jose Mercury News_ covered the election of professor emeritus of environmental studies **Michael Soule** to the American Academy of Arts and Sciences.

Research on climate change by **Lisa Sloan**, professor of Earth sciences, was mentioned in a long article on the environment in _Congressional Quarterly Weekly_.

A study on the effects of ocean warming on elephant seals by **Burney Le Boeuf,** professor emeritus of ecology and evolutionary biology, was covered on various web sites, including _LiveScience, Innovations Report, ScienceBlog, SciScoop_, and _WildlifeNews_.

Professor emeritus of economics **Richard Musgrave** was mentioned in a (Worcester, Mass.) _Sunday Telegram_ article about economist Attiat Ott of Clark University, who described Musgrave as her true mentor.
