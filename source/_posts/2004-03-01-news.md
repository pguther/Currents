UCSC in the News

Economist **Ken Kletzer**'s views about collective action clauses, which have been heralded as a breakthrough in the sovereign debt restructuring crisis, were featured in the December issue of _Latin Finance._ . . . Also in economics, **Lori Kletzer**'s proposal to provide wage insurance to workers who are displaced by foreign competition was outlined in a _New York Times_ article about foreign competition and job loss.  

The_ San Francisco Chronicle_ ran a feature story on the innovative architecture collective, The Ant Farm, noting that it was cofounded by **Chip Lord,** chair of the Film and Digital Media Department.

Psychology's **Craig Haney** was quoted in a _Tampa Tribune_ article about the needs of people released from death row and the lack of support programs to help them make the transition to life outside.  

The _Contra Costa Times_ published an article about music lecturer (and San Francisco Ballet concertmaster) **Roy Malan** after he was named concertmaster of the California Symphony.
