Astronomer **Steve Vogt** was quoted in a _Chicago Tribune_ article about the discovery of an extrasolar planet with potentially Earthlike characteristics.

**Manuel Pastor **of Latin American and Latino studies penned an opinion piece for the _Santa Cruz Sentinel _about efforts to link development of green industries to greater opportunities for the poor and people of color.

An article in _New Scientist_ magazine about the sequencing of the rhesus macaque genome included a quote from **David Haussler**, professor of biomolecular engineering.

The _Salinas Californian _wrote about the new book by **Ryan Coonerty **of legal studies, who has published a coffee-table book about the nation's monuments.

Dream expert **Veronica Tonay **of psychology was featured in a _Sacramento Bee _article about bad dreams that got widespread media play through the Scripps Howard News Service, appearing in the _Mobile Register_, the _Monterey Herald_, and many other papers.

Astronomer **Francis Nimmo** was featured in a _New Scientist_ story about the effects of an ancient impact on the moon.

The _Santa Cruz Sentinel _interviewed **Paul Ortiz **of community studies for their coverage of the Reel Work May Day Labor Film Festival. Ortiz directs the annual festival.

**Glenn Stewart** and other researchers with the UCSC Predatory Bird Research Group continued to get media attention for their work with nesting peregrine falcons, the latest round including stories in the _Santa Cruz Sentinel, San Jose Mercury News,_ Associated Press, and the web sites of TV stations KGO and KPIX, while Stewart has also been working with a reporter for National Public Radio and producers for KQED TV and Radio.

The _Sacramento Bee _featured the work of **Paul Frymer **of legal studies in a story about the controversy over remarks by radio celebrity Don Imus.   
  
_Space Daily _is the latest publication to cover the work of **Chris Wilmers **of environmental studies.

The _San Jose Mercury News _and _Fortune Small Business _picked up on a study by economist **Rob Fairlie **about the number of new companies launched in Silicon Valley in the past five years.
