<p>
  <br>
  The grand opening of the <b>Seymour Center</b> at Long Marine Laboratory attracted widespread media attention, including articles in the <i>Sacramento Bee, Monterey County Herald, San Jose Mercury News, Santa Cruz County Sentinel, Metro Santa Cruz,</i> and <i>Good Times.</i> Broadcast coverage included radio stations KSCO and KUSP and TV stations KSBW and KION.
</p>
<p>
  <b>Kevin Cashen</b> of Media Services was on KUSP's <i>First Person Singular</i> discussing the invention the wheel and the possible connection between wheels and the thrill of speed that feeds mindless aggression.
</p>
<p>
  Psychology's <b>Craig Haney</b> was interviewed by a crew from A&amp;E Television for a report on conditions in supermaximum security prisons.
</p>
<p>
  <b>Barbara Rogoff</b> of psychology and <b>Daniel Press</b> of environmental studies appeared on KUSP's <i>Talk of the Bay</i> show last week to discuss narrative evaluations.<br>
  <br>
  Associate professor of biology <b>Pete Raimondi</b> was quoted in the <i>San Luis Obispo Telegram-Tribune</i> in a story about the biological impacts of the Diablo Canyon nuclear power plant. Raimondi is a member of a technical team reviewing data on the effects on marine life of the plant's cooling system.<br>
