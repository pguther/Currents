Psychology professor Aida Hurtado was the keynote speaker during events at Evergreen Valley College in San Jose celebrating Latino Heritage Month. Hurtado also attended a lunch with Evergreen Chancellor Rosa G. Perez, Evergreen President David Wain Coon, student leaders, and event coordinators.
