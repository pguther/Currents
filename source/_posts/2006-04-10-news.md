<p>
  <i>Salon.com</i> quoted a speech by assistant professor of literature <b>Vilashini Cooppan</b> in an article about how motherhood affects the careers of women in academia.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Silicon Valley Business Journal</i> ran a story about a project to develop new biosensor technology led by electrical engineer <b>Holger Schmidt</b>.
</p>
<p>
  Research by postdoctoral researcher <b>Myra Finkelstein</b> on increasing levels of environmental contaminants in albatrosses was reported by <i>Science Now</i>.
</p>
<p>
  <b>David Haussler</b>, professor of biomolecular engineering, was featured in a <i>Wired</i> magazine story about his work to reconstruct the genomes of extinct mammalian ancestors.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Edmonton Journal</i> ran a story about history of consciousness professor <b>Angela Davis</b> in advance of an appearance at the University of Alberta, where she delivered a lecture titled "Reforming Race, Class and Gender in the 21st Century."
</p>
<p>
  <b>Shakespeare Santa Cruz</b> and UCSC's student-run theater company <b>BarnStorm</b> received gold and silver awards respectively in <i>Metro Santa Cruz</i>'s Readers' Choice Goldies Awards. Theater arts lecturer <b>Tandy Beal</b> also won a silver award for best dance company.
</p>
