_Salon.com_ quoted a speech by assistant professor of literature **Vilashini Cooppan** in an article about how motherhood affects the careers of women in academia.

The _Silicon Valley Business Journal_ ran a story about a project to develop new biosensor technology led by electrical engineer **Holger Schmidt**.

Research by postdoctoral researcher **Myra Finkelstein** on increasing levels of environmental contaminants in albatrosses was reported by _Science Now_.

**David Haussler**, professor of biomolecular engineering, was featured in a _Wired_ magazine story about his work to reconstruct the genomes of extinct mammalian ancestors.

The _Edmonton Journal_ ran a story about history of consciousness professor **Angela Davis** in advance of an appearance at the University of Alberta, where she delivered a lecture titled "Reforming Race, Class and Gender in the 21st Century."

**Shakespeare Santa Cruz** and UCSC's student-run theater company **BarnStorm** received gold and silver awards respectively in _Metro Santa Cruz_'s Readers' Choice Goldies Awards. Theater arts lecturer **Tandy Beal** also won a silver award for best dance company.
