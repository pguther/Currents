<p>
  Cliff Anderson, director of development research, received a 2005 California Advancement Researchers Association (CARA) Service Award in recognition of his contributions to the development research profession. Anderson is immediate past president of CARA, and a speaker on topics related to fundraising research.
</p>
<p>
  The award was presented during the Association of Professional Researchers for Advancement international conference in San Diego in August.
