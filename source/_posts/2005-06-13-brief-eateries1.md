---

**Joe's Pizza and Subs** will be open from 11 a.m. to 5 p.m., Monday through Friday.

**Stevenson Coffee House** will be open from 8 a.m. to 3 p.m., Monday through Thursday, and 8 a.m. to 2 p.m. on Friday.

**Kresge Cafe** will be open from 11 a.m. to 2:30 p.m., Monday through Friday.

The **University Center** will be open one day a week for lunch beginning the week of June 20 when University Dining Services takes over for Sodexho. A buffet lunch will be served on Wednesdays from 11:30 a.m. to 2 p.m. at a cost of $9.95, including beverage.
