The _San Francisco Chronicle_ reviewed Shakespeare Santa Cruz's performances of _Twelfth Night_ and _Engaged,_ noting that they showcased "the ongoing vigor of this annual festival" and "the canny leadership of Artistic Director **Paul Whitworth."** The _Chronicle_ also quoted Whitworth in a feature story in the "Sunday Datebook" section that included a full-page photo from _Engaged._

A major grant from the Moore Foundation to establish a marine microbiology lab at UCSC was covered in the _San Jose Mercury News, Santa Cruz Sentinel, Aptos Times,_ and _Monterey County Herald,_ with quotes from **Jonathan Zehr,** professor of ocean sciences, and **Gary Griggs,** director of the Institute for Marine Sciences.

History of Consciousness professor **Angela Davis** was quoted in _The Age, Australia_ about her work as a civil rights activist and her appearance at a Melbourne conference.

**James Zachos,** professor of Earth sciences, was quoted in a story about climate change that ran in major Canadian newspapers, including the _Edmonton Post, Montreal Gazette, Victoria Times Colonist, Leader-Post,_ and _Star Phoenix._

_Metro Santa Cruz _ran a feature story offering a "behind-the-scenes" look at **Shakespeare Santa Cruz. **

_Environment News_ ran a story about research by biologist **Burney Le Boeuf** on the effects of ocean temperatures on elephant seal pups.

**Raphael Kudela,** associate professor of ocean sciences, was quoted in a story about harmful algal blooms in the _Christian Science Monitor_ and _USA Today._

The _Santa Cruz Sentinel_ extensively quoted Shakespeare Santa Cruz director and theater arts professor **Paul Whitworth** in a feature about the new summer season.

An article in the _San Jose Mercury News_ about unusual conditions along the West Coast this year included a quote from marine biology graduate student **Michael Weise.**

The _Santa Cruz Good Times_ ran a story about the 25th anniversary of the UCSC Dickens Project, noting that it was cofounded by literature professor **John Jordan** and professor of English and comparative literature **Murray Baumgarten.**

An article in the _San Jose Mercury News_ about minorities in the sciences featured **Claudia Flores,** a graduate student in Earth sciences, and **Martha Zuniga,** professor of molecular, cell and developmental biology.

An article in _Playboy_ magazine about natural disasters featured asteroid expert **Erik Asphaug** and tsunami expert **Steven Ward. **

Stories in _New Scientist _magazine, _CNN.com, Space.com,_ and U.P.I. wire service about a new study of the asteroid Eros included quotes from **Erik Asphaug,** associate professor of Earth sciences.

Arts & Lectures director **Michelle Witt** was quoted in the _Santa Cruz Sentinel_ about the new 2005-06 performing arts season.

An article in _New Scientist_ magazine about the effects of commercial whaling on killer whales included quotes from biologist **James Estes.**

The _Santa Cruz Sentinel_ reviewed the opening night of **Shakespeare Santa Cruz's** _**Twelfth Night,**_ calling it a "splendid, hilarious production."

An article in _New Scientist_ magazine about problems in physics and cosmology related to "dark energy" included a quote from physicist **Thomas Banks. **

Chemistry professor **Jin Zhang's** research on hydrogen fuel technology was featured in the _Aptos Times._
