#### Films are shown on Monday nights starting at 7 p.m. in Kresge 321

**October 2:** G. W. Pabst (Brecht/Weill): _Die Dreigroschenoper_ (_The Threepenny Opera),_ 1931.

**October 9:** Michael Powell and Emeric Pressburger (Jacques Offenbach): _The Tales of Hoffman,_ 1951.

**October 16:** Ingmar Bergman (W. A. Mozart): _Trollflojten_ (_The Magic Flute),_ 1975.

**October 23:** Jean-Pierre Ponnelle (Mozart/da Ponte): _Le Nozze di Figaro_ (_The Marriage of Figaro),_ 1976.

**October 30:** Patrice Chereau (Richard Wagner): _Das Rheingold,_ 1980.

**November 6:** Joseph Losey (Mozart/da Ponte): _Don Giovanni,_ 1979.

**November 13:** Franco Zefferelli (Giuseppe Verdi): _La Traviata,_ 1982.

**November 20:** Francesco Rosi (Georges Bizet): _Carmen,_ 1984.

**November 27:** Peter Sellars (Mozart/da Ponte): _Cosi fan tutte,_ 1991.

**December 6, 8-12 p.m., Special Exam Period Showing:** Hans-Jurgen Syberberg (Richard Wagner): _Parsifal,_ 1982.
