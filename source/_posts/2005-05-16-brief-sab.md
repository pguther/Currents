The Staff Advisory Board (SAB) is seeking nominations for three open spots on the board. The SAB advises the chancellor on staff issues, nominates staff representatives for committees, helps organize and supports staff appreciation activities, and sends two members of the SAB to quarterly meetings of the systemwide Council of UC Staff Assemblies. The mission of the board and the duties of board membership are explained in detail on the [SAB web site][1].

All nonrepresented career employees are eligible to serve on the SAB. Nominations are due May 16; download a [nomination form][2]. The term for new members begins July 1.

For more information, contact the SAB at [staffadv@ucsc.edu][3].
