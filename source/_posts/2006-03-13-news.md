<a name="content" id="content"></a>
<p>
  Astrophysicist <b>Stan Woosley</b> was quoted in stories in the <i>Washington Post</i> and <i>Philadelphia Inquirer</i> about a recently detected gamma-ray burst.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Washington Post</i> reviewed a new book by history professor <b>Bruce Levine</b>, <i>Confederate Emancipation: Southern Plans to Free and Arm Slaves During the Civil War,</i> calling it "brilliantly researched and persuasively argued."
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> ran a story about <b>Mary Silver</b>, professor of ocean sciences, and her research on algal toxins in Monterey Bay.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Honolulu Advertiser</i> quoted art lecturer <b>Paul Rangell</b> in a story about the Honolulu Printmakers 78th Annual Exhibition for which he served as juror and visiting artist.
</p>
<p>
  An article in the <i>Mid-County Post</i> on the long-range development plan for Long Marine Lab included quotes from LML manager <b>Steve Davenport</b> and Seymour Center director <b>Julie Barrett Heffington</b>.
</p>
<p>
  University Librarian <b>Virginia Steel</b> was quoted in a <i>Register-Pajaronian</i> article about a $100,000 gift from the Hugh Stuart Center Charitable Trust in San Jose to create a new California History Room in McHenry Library's Special Collections. The <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> also announced the gift.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> ran an article about upcoming performances by New York's David Dorfman Dance company, noting the collaboration between UCSC's <b>Arts &amp; Lectures</b> program and the Cultural Council of Santa Cruz to bring arts education into the community.
</p>
