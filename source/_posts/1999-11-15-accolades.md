#### Mark Franko

Professor of Theater Arts (Dance) Mark Franko presented a paper, titled "The Political Figure in Dance Studies," in Leuven, Belgium, for a conference, "The Salt of the Earth: On Dance, Politics, and Reality," sponsored by the Klapstuk Dance Festival and the Flemish Theatre Institute. While in Belgium, he also conducted a seminar on "Dance and Radicalism" at the Institute for Cultural Studies of the Katholieke Universiteit Leuven. He was also respondent for a panel on "Music and Military Culture in Early Modern Europe" at the American Musicological Society.
