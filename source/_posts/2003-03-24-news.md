UCSC in the News

Several profs have fielded war-related queries from the media lately, including** Isebill Gruhn** of politics who was interviewed by KGO Radio, and **Anthony Pratkanis,** whose expertise on wartime propaganda and psychological warfare has been in demand. He has fielded calls from the _South China Morning Post,_ Hong Kong's biggest English-language newspaper, as well as from the _Los Angeles Times._

The _New York Times_ ran an op-ed piece by American studies professor **Tricia Rose** about the effect of race, sex, and stigmas in the fight against AIDS.

Chancellor **M.R.C. Greenwood** was quoted in an article in the _San Francisco Chronicle_ about new federal rules governing research on potential bioterror agents.

The _New York Times_ devoted several paragraphs in an article on the front page of the Health & Fitness section to discussing the book _Lack of Character: Personality and Moral Behavior_ by associate professor of philosophy **John Doris**.

Economist **Nirvikar Singh** was quoted in a _Computer User_ article about jobs, and economist **Lori Kletzer** appeared in the winter issue of _Region Focus,_ discussing free-trade issues.

The Arboretum's **Stephen McCabe** was featured in an article in the _Santa Cruz Sentinel_ about Hummingbird Day at the Arboretum.

Graduate students **Teresa Ish** and **Shelly Benoit** were featured in a _Santa Cruz Sentinel_ article about their efforts to educate consumers about how to choose sustainably harvested fish.  

History of conscious professor **Angela Davis** was quoted in the _Boston Globe_ about an appearance at Harvard's Kennedy School of Government, where she argued that prisons should be abolished. She was also quoted in the Lancaster, Pa., _Intelligencer Journal_ about her critique of the Bush administration in an appearance at Millersville University.

Astronomer** Stephen Thorsett** was quoted in a UPI wire service story about pulsars.
