UCSC in the News

**Bruce Lyon,** assistant professor of ecology and evolutionary biology, was quoted in an article in _New Scientist_ magazine about the counting abilities of animals.

The _New York Time_s quoted Film and Digital Media Department chair and professor **Chip Lord** in an article about the Ant Farm--the innovative art and architecture collective he co-founded that is now being celebrated with a retrospective at the Berkeley Art Museum. The _East Bay Express_ also ran a story on Lord's background.  

The _Salinas Californian_ reviewed a new book by literature professor **Julianne Burton-Carvajal** about Central Coast artist Jo Mora.   

The _Monterey County Herald_ noted that acclaimed photographer **Al Weber** is a volunteer adviser to the UCSC Library's Special Collections in an article about a new exhibit of Weber's photographs at the Monterey Museum of Art.   

American studies professor and department chair **Tricia Rose** was quoted in the _Contra Costa Times_ about the influence of pop culture on clothing choices.
