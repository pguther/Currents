  UCSC in the News
</p>
<p>
  <b>Bruce Lyon,</b> assistant professor of ecology and evolutionary biology, was quoted in an article in <i>New Scientist</i> magazine about the counting abilities of animals.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>New York Time</i>s quoted Film and Digital Media Department chair and professor <b>Chip Lord</b> in an article about the Ant Farm--the innovative art and architecture collective he co-founded that is now being celebrated with a retrospective at the Berkeley Art Museum. The <i>East Bay Express</i> also ran a story on Lord's background.<br>
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Salinas Californian</i> reviewed a new book by literature professor <b>Julianne Burton-Carvajal</b> about Central Coast artist Jo Mora.<br>
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Monterey County Herald</i> noted that acclaimed photographer <b>Al Weber</b> is a volunteer adviser to the UCSC Library's Special Collections in an article about a new exhibit of Weber's photographs at the Monterey Museum of Art.<br>
</p>
<p>
  American studies professor and department chair <b>Tricia Rose</b> was quoted in the <i>Contra Costa Times</i> about the influence of pop culture on clothing choices.
