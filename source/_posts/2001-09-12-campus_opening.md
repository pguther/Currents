**Events planned as fall quarter gets under way**

THURSDAY, SEPTEMBER 13

* **"A Moment of Reflection," a campus community gathering in response to recent terrorist attacks. **All staff, faculty, students and their friends and family welcome, Music Recital Hall, noon to 1 p.m.
* **Move-In and Opening** of Merrill College

  
FRIDAY, SEPTEMBER 14

* **Move-In and Opening** of Oakes College, College Nine, Crown College, and Kresge College

  
SATURDAY, SEPTEMBER 15

* **Move-In and Opening** of College Eight, Porter College, Cowell College, and Stevenson College

SUNDAY, SEPTEMBER 16

* **Graduate Student Reception,** 3 to 5 p.m., University House
* **Candlelight Vigil to remember victims of terrorist attacks,** Upper Quarry, early evening

  
MONDAY, SEPTEMBER 17

* **Dedication of MBEST** (Monterey Bay Education, Science and Technology Center) in Marina, 10 a.m.
* **Student Orientation Activities**

  
TUESDAY, SEPTEMBER 18

* **Boardwalk Night **for undergraduates and other members of the campus community
* **Student Orientation Activities**

  
WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER 19

* **Classes begin**
