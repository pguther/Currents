Polls will be open from 7 a.m. to 8 p.m. Tuesday for the gubernatorial primary election.

Polling places on campus are:

* Oakes College: Learning Center  
* Cowell College Apartments: Community Room  
* Crown/Merrill Apartments: Community Room  
* Porter College Apartments: I Lounge  
* College Nine: Red Room, Rm 201  
* College Ten: Namaste Lounge  
* Stevenson College: Silverman Conference Room

Additional information is available at the Santa Cruz County [Elections Department web site. ][1]
