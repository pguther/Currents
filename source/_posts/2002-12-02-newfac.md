New Faculty

Katia Obraczka  
Assistant Professor of Computer Engineering

Katia Obraczka studies computer networks, distributed systems, Internet information systems, and operating systems. She earned a B.S. in electrical engineering and M.S. in computer engineering from the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, and M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in computer science from the University of Southern California. Before joining the UCSC faculty, Obraczka was an adjunct research professor of computer science at USC.
