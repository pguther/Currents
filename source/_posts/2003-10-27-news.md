UCSC in the News

**Slawek Tulaczyk,** associate professor of Earth sciences, was featured in a _Los Angeles Times_ article about changes in California's glaciers. While Sierra Nevada glaciers are in rapid retreat, those on Mt. Shasta are growing. The story also ran in the _San Francisco Chronicle, San Mateo County Times, Boston Globe,_ and _Dallas-Fort Worth Star Telegram._

Chancellor **M.R.C. Greenwood** and **Nancy Aebersold, **director of the Higher Education Recruitment Consortium (HERC), were quoted in a _San Jose Mercury_ _News_ article about the launch of the first northern California web site for jobs in higher education -- founded by UCSC. Articles also appeared in the _San Francisco Chronicle, San Jose Business Journal, Contra Costa Times_ and_ Santa Cruz Sentinel,_ and the story was additionally covered by KGO, KLIV, and KSCO radio.

UCSC was mentioned in a pair of articles in the _San Francisco Chronicle_ about efforts to foster interdisciplinary collaborations at top universities. **Michael Isaacson,** associate dean of research and graduate studies in the engineering school, was quoted in one of the stories.

The _New York Times_ profiled American studies professor and department chair **Tricia Rose** in an article titled "Class with the 'Ph.D. Diva.'"  

Adjunct professor of computer science **Arthur Keller** was featured in a segment on the _News Hour_ with Jim Lehrer about Wi-Fi (wireless Internet) networks.  

KSBW-TV interviewed statistician **David Draper** for a story about the California Lottery.  

A story in the _Silicon Valley/San Jose Business Journal_ about UCSC's partnership with NASA Ames in a University Affiliated Research Center included quotes from Chancellor **M.R.C. Greenwood** and **William Berry,** deputy director of the Silicon Valley Center.   

**Daniel Costa,** professor of ecology and evolutionary biology, was quoted in a story about the effects of sonar on whales that ran in a large number of newspapers, including the _Contra Costa Times, Detroit Free Press, Houston Chronicle, Lexington Herald Leader, Miami Herald, Pittsburgh Post-Gazette, Saint Paul Pioneer Press, San Jose Mercury News,_ and _San Diego Union Tribune._ Costa was also quoted in a related story that ran in the _San Francisco Chronicle._  
  
The _Santa Cruz Sentinel_ ran an article quoting music lecturer **Irene Herrmann** about _Paul Bowles on Music,_ a new book she coedited of music criticism by the renowned author.
