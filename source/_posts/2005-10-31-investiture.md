Chancellor Denice Denton joined the celebration of Dia de los Muertos on October 25 at the Museum of Art & History in Santa Cruz, one of several public events leading up to her investiture on Friday, November 4, as UCSC's ninth chancellor.

See [press release][1] for information on the academic symposium on Thursday and the investiture.
