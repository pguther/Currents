E. Melanie DuPuis, associate professor of sociology, received the 2005 Frederick H. Buttel Outstanding Scholarship Award from the Rural Sociological Society in recognition of her book, _Nature's Perfect Food_.

The award was presented during the association's annual meeting in Tampa in August.
