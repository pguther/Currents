Get out of the office and de-stress, burn calories, build muscles, reduce blood pressure, and meet new people. Join the Staff and Faculty Walking Club, which meets for the first time this academic year on October 18. Participants at all levels of fitness are welcome. Come once a week or more. Groups walk during the lunch hour on the following days:

* Tuesdays, meet at noon in front of the Student Health Center. Walk led by Meg Kobe.
* Thursdays, meet at noon in front of the Student Health Center. Walk led by Jane Bogart.
* Fridays, meet at noon at the Hahn loading dock. Walk led by Susan Girard.

For more information, call Meg Kobe at (831) 459-3772, Jane Bogart at (831) 459-4679, or Susan Girard at (831) 459-2275. To receive club updates by e-mail, contact Cindy Mori at [camori@ucsc.edu][1] or (831) 459-5781.
