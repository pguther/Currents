Economist **Mike Dooley **was featured in the _Financial Times_. Dooley has cofounded a capital management group that specializes in emerging markets, in keeping with his analysis of the global economy.

The _San Francisco Chronicle_ featured literature professor **Nathaniel Mackey** in a story/interview about his work as a poet.

In an article about Korean educators impressed by the "open classroom" philosophy of a local school, the _Salt Lake City Tribune_ cited a book by psychology's **Barbara Rogoff**.

The _Pantagraph_ ran a story about a talk that history of consciousness professor **Angela Davis** gave at Illinois Wesleyan University in celebration of Black History Month.

The _Santa Cruz Sentinel_ ran a front-page story about UCSC's standing as a leader in the national spoken-word movement; the campus's poetry slam team won the gold medal at the national college championships in 2005 and last week hosted a series of workshops and performances.
