<p>
  Economist <strong>Mike Dooley</strong> was featured in the <i>Financial Times</i>. Dooley has cofounded a capital management group that specializes in emerging markets, in keeping with his analysis of the global economy.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>San Francisco Chronicle</i> featured literature professor <strong>Nathaniel Mackey</strong> in a story/interview about his work as a poet.
</p>
<p>
  In an article about Korean educators impressed by the "open classroom" philosophy of a local school, the <i>Salt Lake City Tribune</i> cited a book by psychology's <strong>Barbara Rogoff</strong>.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Pantagraph</i> ran a story about a talk that history of consciousness professor <strong>Angela Davis</strong> gave at Illinois Wesleyan University in celebration of Black History Month.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Santa Cruz Sentinel</i> ran a front-page story about UCSC's standing as a leader in the national spoken-word movement; the campus's poetry slam team won the gold medal at the national college championships in 2005 and last week hosted a series of workshops and performances.
</p>
