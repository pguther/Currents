<p>
  A story about high-tech animal tagging in the <i>San Diego Union-Tribune</i> featured biologists <b>Scott Shaffer</b> and <b>Daniel Costa.</b>
</p>
<p>
  A <i>Sunset</i> magazine item on "5 great stores for creative gifts" included <b>Norrie's Gift Shop</b> at the Arboretum, along with a photo of the shop (see <a href="brief-norries.asp">Currents article</a> on Norrie's December discount for faculty and staff).
</p>
<p>
  Astrophysicist <b>Stan Woosley</b> was quoted in a <i>Science Now</i> article about the discovery of a rapidly spinning black hole.
</p>
<p>
  India's <i>The Statesman</i> noted that UCSC's <b>Satyajit Ray Film and Study Collection</b> provided materials for a film festival and exhibition in Spain honoring the renowned film director.
</p>
<p>
  The <i>Mid-County Post</i> ran a story about research on red tides by ocean scientists <b>Raphael Kudela</b> and <b>Mary Silver.</b>
</p>
<p>
  Geologist <b>Gary Griggs</b> and geophysicist <b>Steven Ward</b> were featured in a <i>Mid-County Post</i> article about the minor tsunami that rocked boats in the Santa Cruz Yacht Harbor.
</p>
<p>
  Pennsylvania's <i>The Morning Call</i> quoted history of consciousness professor <b>Angela Davis</b> in an article about a lecture she presented at Lafayette's Colton Chapel titled, "Reflecting on our History: How Activism and Diversity Changed Higher Education."
</p>
