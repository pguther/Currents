<p>
  Biologist <b>Don Croll</b> was featured on the radio progam <i>Pulse of the Planet</i> in a segment about Croll's research on penguins in Antarctica. <i>Pulse of the Planet</i> is broadcast on public and commercial radio stations around the world, including KUSP, KQED, KALW, and KBOQ.<br>
  <br>
  A story in the <i>Santa Cruz County Sentinel</i> about the restoration of Long Marine Lab's blue whale skeleton and its reassembly at the Seymour Center featured quotes from campus veterinarian <b>Dave Casper</b>, Seymour Center director <b>Julie Barrett Heffington</b>, and Long Marine Lab manager <b>Steve Davenport</b>. The skeleton is being reassembled in preparation for the opening of the center in March. Heffington was also quoted in a <i>Sentinel</i> story about the formal dedication of the Seymour Center on January 15.
