UCSC in the News

The _Oakland Tribune_ did a feature article about a UC study of first-year students that's being conducted by psychology's **Margarita Azmitia.**

The Associated Press, _Santa Cruz Sentinel,_ and _San Jose Mercury News_ were among the media outlets that reported on the faculty approval of an antiwar resolution sponsored by **Paul Ortiz** of community studies.

**Michael Riordan,** adjunct professor of physics, was quoted in a _New York Times_ article about a German physicist who independently invented the transistor shortly after the Bell Laboratories researchers who won fame and Nobel Prizes for the invention.

Astronomer** Puragra (Raja) GuhaThakurta** was featured in an article in the _Mid-County Post_ about research in astronomy at UCSC.   

**Lindsay Hinck,** assistant professor of molecular, cell, and developmental biology, and **John Tamkun,** professor and chair of MCD biology, were quoted in a _Santa Cruz Sentinel_ article about UCSC's new health sciences major.

An article in_ Good Times_ about coastal erosion and seawalls featured the comments of **Gary Griggs,** director of the Institute of Marine Sciences, and graduate student **Dawn Osborn**.  

**Harry Noller,** Sinsheimer Professor of Molecular Biology, was interviewed on the KUSP Radio program _Talk of the Bay_ on the subject of UCSC's role in the Human Genome Project.
