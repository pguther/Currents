09 October 2000

'Opera and Film' screening schedule

Public Information Office

Films are shown on Monday nights starting at 7 p.m. in Kresge 321

October 2: G. W. Pabst (Brecht/Weill): Die Dreigroschenoper (The Threepenny Opera), 1931.

October 9: Michael Powell and Emeric Pressburger (Jacques Offenbach): The Tales of Hoffman, 1951.

October 16: Ingmar Bergman (W. A. Mozart): Trollflojten (The Magic Flute), 1975.

October 23: Jean-Pierre Ponnelle (Mozart/da Ponte): Le Nozze di Figaro (The Marriage of Figaro), 1976.

October 30: Patrice Chereau (Richard Wagner): Das Rheingold, 1980.

November 6: Joseph Losey (Mozart/da Ponte): Don Giovanni, 1979.

November 13: Franco Zefferelli (Giuseppe Verdi): La Traviata, 1982.

November 20: Francesco Rosi (Georges Bizet): Carmen, 1984.

November 27: Peter Sellars (Mozart/da Ponte): Cosi fan tutte, 1991.

December 6, 8-12 p.m., Special Exam Period Showing: Hans-Jurgen Syberberg (Richard Wagner): Parsifal, 1982.
