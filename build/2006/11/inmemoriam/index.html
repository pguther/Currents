15 November 2006

Memorial service scheduled for Nicholas Sverchek

Public Information Department

A memorial service is planned for Nicholas Sverchek, a Stevenson College student who died November 2 while studying at the University of Delhi as part of UC's Education Abroad Program (see campus message).

The service will be held in Bakersfield on Saturday, November 18, at 3 p.m. Details of the event are available online in an obituary in the Bakersfield Californian (obituary).
