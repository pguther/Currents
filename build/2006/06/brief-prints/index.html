Annual student print sale at Baskin Visual Arts June 9-10

Public Information Department

[caption id=”attachment_5101” align=”alignnone” width=”200”]Irene Hamaker, 2006[/caption]

June 5, 2006

The 32nd Annual Print Sale is an extraordinary display of undergraduate artwork and a showcase for the printmaking program and facilities at UCSC. Hundreds of original prints will be for sale in a variety of media.

The event will run in conjunction with the Art Department's Spring Quarter Open Studios. Friday, June 9, and Saturday, June 10, 10 a.m. to 6 p.m., at Baskin Visual Arts. For further information, contact Veda Ozelle at (831) 459-3686 or go to arts.ucsc.edu/printsale/index.html.
