03 April 2006

UCSC senior custodian honored as 'Woman of the Year'

Public Information Department

[caption id=”attachment_4801” align=”alignnone” width=”230”]John Laird, left, with UCSC custodian Maria Elena Alcantar and Fabian Nunez, Assembly Speaker[/caption]

UCSC senior custodian Maria Elena Alcantar has been named Woman of the Year for the 27th Assembly District by Assemblyman John Laird. Each year, assembly members select one of their constituents for Woman of the Year honors.

"I'm inspired by Maria Elena Alcantar's focus on creating a brighter future for her family and her coworkers," said Laird. "Maria Elena has worked tirelessly to improve working conditions at UCSC while building education opportunities for her children."

Laird recognized Alcantar on the floor of the Assembly in a March 20 ceremony.
