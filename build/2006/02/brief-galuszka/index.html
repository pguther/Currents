13 February 2006

Art professor exhibits paintings in New York and Philadelphia

Public Information Department

[caption id=”attachment_4612” align=”alignnone” width=”340”]<img class=”size-full wp-image-4612” src=”http://localhost/mysite/wp-content/uploads/2006/02/big.cliff.340.jpg” alt=”“Big Cliff” by Frank Galuszka” width=”340” height=”242” />“Big Cliff” by Frank Galuszka[/caption]

Art professor and department chair Frank Galuszka has two exhibitions of his paintings running on the East Coast this winter. The New York Studio School of Painting and Sculpture is displaying his works through February 16 and the Projects Gallery in Philadelphia is featuring his paintings through March 12.

Galuszka joined the UCSC faculty in 1995 and is now one of the area's premier plein air painters. He is also on the executive committee of UCSC's Arts Research Institute. For more information about his work, go to arts.ucsc.edu/faculty/fgaluszka.
