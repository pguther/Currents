Job networking event for students Jan. 18

Public Information Department

The Career Center is hosting a Nonprofit and Social Service Networking Event on Wednesday, January 18, from 1 to 4 p.m. at the Stevenson Events Center.

The event is open to all registered students with ID. Students are advised to bring resumes. For a complete listing of participating employers go to www2.ucsc.edu/careers/events/npf.html
