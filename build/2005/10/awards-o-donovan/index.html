17 October 2005

Staff member Connell O'Donovan wins prize for essay

Public Information Department

Connell O'Donovan, program manager for UCSC Summer Session, won the first-place award this year in an annual writing contest sponsored by the organization Affirmation: Gay and Lesbian Mormons.

O'Donovan received a $1,000 prize for his essay, titled "'Losing My Religion' - or, How I Baked a Custard Pudding and Lost My Belief in Mormonism." The award was announced October 4 at the annual Affirmation General Conference, held this year in Denver.
