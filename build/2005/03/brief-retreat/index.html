28 March 2005

Women at Work Retreat has openings

Public Information Department

The Women at Work Retreat is still accepting applications for the upcoming April 21-22 retreat in San Juan Bautista.

This year's theme is the Art of Adapting to Change: Juggling Tradition and Innovation.

Space is limited; applications will be accepted until registration is filled. For more information or an application, contact Dale Whyte at (831) 459-5652, whyte@ucsc.edu, or Laura McCann at (831) 459-2228, llmccann@ucsc.edu.
