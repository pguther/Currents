Reception for Acting Chancellor Chemers

Public Information Department

[caption id=”attachment_3382” align=”alignnone” width=”345”]Margaret Delaney, interim campus provost and executive vice chancellor, was among those honoring outgoing Acting Chancellor Martin M. Chemers at a Jan. 28 reception at University Center. Members of the campus community were joined by elected officials, including Santa Cruz City Councilwoman Emily Reilly, State Assemblyman John Laird, and former State Senator Bruce McPherson. Ken Feingold, president of the UC Santa Cruz Foundation, and UC Alumni Regent Gary Novack also spoke to praise Chemers.
