UCSC in the News

Chemistry professors Bakthan Singaram and Todd Wipke were quoted in news stories about an optical glucose sensor developed in Singaram's lab. Stories appeared in the Washington Times, U.P.I. news wire, Medical News Today, Medical News U.K., WebIndia.com, and the Santa Cruz Sentinel.

The Santa Cruz Sentinel ran a lengthy interview with Chancellor M.R.C. Greenwood covering the successes and challenges of her eight years at UCSC and her expectations for her new position with UC President Robert Dynes.

American studies professor and department chair Tricia Rose was quoted in the Chicago Tribune and Denver Post about the influence of pop star Britney Spears.

The Santa Cruz Sentinel noted that music lecturer Robert Klevan was recently named as jazz education director for the Monterey Jazz Festival.

The Register-Pajaronian published a lengthy review of music lecturer Mesut Ozgen's multimedia concert featuring assistant film and digital media professor Gustavo Vazquez, music lecturer Peter Elsea, and assistant theater arts professor David Cuthbert, at the Mello Center in Watsonville.
