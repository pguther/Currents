Celebrate Halloween with the Seymour Center on Oct. 29

Public Information Department

The Seymour Center at Long Marine Lab invites the communty to an evening of family Halloween fun on Friday, October 29, from 6:30 to 9:30 p.m.

Join the costume parade. Hear the tale of the 1924 wreck of La Feliz and the fate of her crew. Take a nighttime tour to discover what the ghost of the whale can tell us. Watch the creatures that come out at night to feed in the aquarium tanks. Make crafts and join in the scavenger hunt to win a prize.

Tickets are $7/adults; $4/students, seniors (60+), or youth (6-16); free for children 5 and under. Members pay $4/adults; $3/students, seniors (60+), or youth (6-16).

Call (831) 459-3800 for more information.
