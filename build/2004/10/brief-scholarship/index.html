11 October 2004

Nominations sought for public service scholarship

Public Information Department

The Donald A. Strauss Scholarship Foundation provides scholarship support to carry out public service projects. The $10,000 scholarships are awarded to junior class level students who have demonstrated an interest in public service, have outstanding leadership potential, have developed and can demonstrate effective communication skills, and wish to make a difference in local, regional, or national communities. More information is available on the web at www.straussfoundation.org.

Nominations and questions may be directed to Dianne Wright in the Chancellor's Office, dwright@ucsc.edu. Nominations are due by January 14.
