25 October 2004

UCSC in the News

Public Information Department

Theater Arts professor and department chair Danny Scheie was quoted about his acting technique in BackStage.

In education, news of Judit Moschkovich and Julia Aguirre's new research project designed to help Latinos learn mathematics made a splash on the front page of the Santa Cruz Sentinel and appeared in the San Jose Mercury News.

The Santa Cruz Sentinel ran an article about a newly published biography of violin legend Efrem Zimbalist written by music department lecturer Roy Malan.

Drug policy expert Craig Reinarman of sociology was quoted in a Contra Costa Times article about an Oakland ballot item that would direct law enforcement officials to give marijuana use the lowest priority.

The Santa Cruz Sentinel noted a class taught on hip-hop culture by American Studies professor Tricia Rose in a piece about a local performance by KRS-One.

Media Highlights provides monthly summaries of "UCSC in the News" columns.
