27 September 2004

Job and internship fair set for October 6

Public Information Department

The Early Bird Job & Internship Fair, sponsored by the Career Center, will be held from 1 to 4:30 p.m. October 6 at the Stevenson Events Center.

Students do not need to sign up in advance for the fair.

Additional information, including participating employers and tips on getting the most out of job fairs, is available online at www2.ucsc.edu/careers/events/
